How to make a Roblox shirt template

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, June 1, 2024

ROBLOX is one of the world's most popular online gaming platforms, where users can hang out with friends, explore virtual spaces and even develop their own games.

Gamers can customize their avatar by creating clothing, like shirts and pants, to give them their own style and uniqueness.

How to make a Roblox shirt template

Users can make their own clothes for their avatar and here we will show you the tricks needed.

Only members of the Roblox Builders Club can make shirts and pants but no membership is required to make a T-shirt.

Clothing can be made for your own avatar to wear but can also be sold via the catalog.

Pick your shirt

First you need to select the image you want.

Go to the Roblox Studio and access the asset manager through the view tab.

A T-shirt is the simplest design to create but fairly basic.

Roblox utilizes images in .png, .jpg, .tga, or .bmp format. 

You need to right-click and choose Save as and open it with an editing software such as Photoshop, GIMP, or MS Paint.

You can also upload your own image but it must be 512x512 pixels.

Bear in mind that the image will be covering your avatar's torso.

Shirts as well as pants can also be created and give you more control and allow you to create your own designs.

In the asset manager select the image you want to create and download it to your computer.

For each template, notice that the parts are "folded up" and wrapped around a Roblox character's body, arms, and legs.

Apply the right dimensions

Following the Roblox clothing instructions, you need to make sure the dimensions are correct.

  • Large square: 128 × 128 pixels for the front and back of your torso.
  • Tall rectangle: 64 × 128 pixels. R and L parts go on the sides of your torso and L, B, R, and F parts go on the sides of your arms and legs.
  • Wide rectangle: 128 × 64 pixels for the top and bottom of your torso.
  • Small square: 64 × 64 pixels for the top and bottom of your arms or legs.

Once your design has all the correct parts, you are good to go.

Upload and save your new clothes

Your next step is to upload your shirt by selecting it on the Roblox create page.

Hit Choose file and then Upload.

Once your shirt is ready it will be added to your creations for your avatar to wear or to be sold to other players.

Be aware though that a fee is charged for some items.

Read more on The Sun

An upload fee needs to be paid when an item is first uploaded, before it is moderated.

A selling fee is paid the first time an item is marked “on sale” and becomes available for others to buy.
