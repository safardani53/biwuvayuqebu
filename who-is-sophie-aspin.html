Who is Sophie Aspin? | The US Sun

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 8, 2024

SOPHIE Aspin first found fame back in 2016 when she went viral for the M to the B song.

The song seems to have resurfaced and gone viral once again, but who is Sophie?

Who is Sophie Aspin?

Sophie Aspin is a grime rapper from Manchester, UK, who then moved to Blackpool as a teenager.

She's the girl spoken about in the infamous song M to the B and recently responded to it going viral once again as she said: "Can someone please tell all these Americans what's going on? They're doing all this, 'M to the B, M to the B' and they're following a B, it's not f****** about a B. It's about me!"

Sophie often documents what she has been getting up to on her Instagram page @sophaspinmusic where she has more than 250k followers.

She has recently given birth to her first child, Ophelia Rose.

At the end of the year Sophie shared a series of photos rounding up her 2022.

She wrote: "Well my 2022 was no doubt the best year of my entire life so far. A rollercoaster of a year is an understatement and I'm coming out of it the happiest I've ever been. I never thought I'd be ending the year in our own home, engaged and with our little girl. Forever Grateful."

What is Sophie Aspin known for?

Sophie became a household name when she was the subject of Millie B's diss-track.

Millie B's 'Soph Aspin Send', which is best known for the iconic line 'It's M to the B' first went viral back in 2016.

The song has recently gone viral again, with many celebrities such as Kim Kardashian singing along to the track.

Is Sophie Aspin on social media?

Yes, Sophie often posts to her Instagram page.

She also regularly uploads to her YouTube channel Soph Aspin Official, where she has more than 25k subscribers.

She tends to share music videos as well as an insight into her life and what she's been up to.

Recently she released her latest song Don't Take It Serious with an official music video.
