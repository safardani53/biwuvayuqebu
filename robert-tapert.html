He catapulted to international prominence as a result of the horror picture. Later on, he started producing films such as “Darkman,” “Crimewave,” “Timecop,” “The Gift,” and others. He has also created television shows such as “Hercules: The Legendary Journey”, “Xena: Warrior Princess”, and “Young Hercules”. Tapert returned to the Evil Dead franchise in 2015, creating the Starz series Ash versus Evil Dead. He is now working on two new drama series, “Airborne” and “Xena”.
