Tamron Hall shocks fans with dramatic hair makeover on first day back to talk show this season

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, May 4, 2024

TALK show host Tamron Hall has shocked fans with a dramatic hair makeover.

Tamron was spotted with platinum blonde hair as she geared up for the season premiere of her talk show "The Tamron Hall Show."

The former NBC News anchor debuted the look on the season premiere in dramatic fashion.

A clip from the show was posted to The Tamron Hall Instagram account.

A video montage played for the live audience, showing the show host getting out of a convertible car and strutting into the building where tapes the show.

Tamron was wearing a strategically-placed head scarf.

As the stage doors open, the show host appears, taking off her head scarf to reveal her hair.

The audience erupted in cheers.

A caption on the post reads: "A very warm welcome from the Tam fam!!

"Living our lives like it's GOLDEN today!"

Her show debuted in 2019 and has since won several Daytime Emmy Awards including one for Outstanding Informative Talk Show Host.

She's interviewed a range of people in the public eye, including Barack Obama before his run for presidency.

Before hosting her own self-titled talk show, she presented on The Today Show, Deadline: Crime with Tamron Hall and NewsNation with Tamron Hall.

Hall has been the host of Deadline: Crime with Tamron Hall since 2013.
