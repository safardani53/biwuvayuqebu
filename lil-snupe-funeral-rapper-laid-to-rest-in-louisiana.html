Rapper Laid to Rest in Louisiana

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, May 14, 2024

Hundreds of mourners gathered this weekend to pay their final respects to the late Lil Snupe at the emerging rapper’s funeral. He was only 18.

According to the Shreveport Times, the funeral service was held Saturday at Jonesboro-Hodge High School in Snupe’s hometown, Jonesboro, La.

Mourners reportedly began arriving three hours before the service began.

Rapper Meek Mill, who had signed Lil Snupe (born Addaren Ross) to his DreamChasers record label last year, was among the many funeral attendees.

Also last week, a 36-year-old man suspected of murdering the teen surrendered to Louisiana police. Tony Holden has been charged with first-degree murder.

RIP Snupe.
