Woman shows off friend's front room with a GIANT soft play frame &amp; people quickly call it 'ridiculou

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, September 26, 2024

A WOMAN sent social media into overdrive when she shared a video of her friend’s big living room transformation. 

And it wasn’t just a lick of paint or a new rug, as the mum had decided to erect a giant soft play frame in her home. 

The short clip, posted by TikTok user @mrs_rowell2be, showed how the multi-coloured kids’ soft play area had taken over half of the space.

And @mrs_rowell2be went on to reveal that the fun mum had bagged it off of Facebook and that she’d originally bought it for her back garden, before moving it inside. 

And she couldn’t get enough of the "cool mum's" idea. 

The TikToker wrote alongside the video, which has since gone viral on social media with over 70k likes: “My friend’s front room is better than your friend’s front room.”
