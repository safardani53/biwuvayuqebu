How old is Bella Ramsey? The Sun

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, May 9, 2024

BELLA Ramsey wowed audiences when she made her debut in season six of Game Of Thrones.

The young actress landed her latest big role, as Ellie in HBO's series, The Last Of Us.

How old is Bella Ramsey?

Bella Ramsey was born in Nottingham, England, in September 2003. 

The 19-year-old actress is best known for her role in Game Of Thrones.

Bella started acting when she was just four years old after joining her local stage school.

When she was ten, she won a place at Television Workshop – an organization that trains talent for stage and screen - and it was there that she decided she wanted to act professionally and so she started to audition for professional TV and film roles.

Bella has played a number of titular characters including Mildred Hubble in the 2017 TV adaptation of The Worst Witch and voiceover for the Netflix show, Hilda. She also starred in Holmes and Watson and His Dark Materials.

Who does Bella Ramsey play in Game Of Thrones?

Bella played Lyanna Mormont in the HBO fantasy drama series, Game of Thrones.

She first appeared in a scene in the seventh episode of the sixth season – The Broken Man - playing the young ruler of the Bear Islands, the niece of the late Lord Commander of the Night’s Watch.

Her role won her rave reviews, with The Hollywood Reporter branding her "season 6's breakout star."

Bella – who previously revealed she can only watch ‘snippets’ of the very adult show - is a big fan of her character.

She said: "Lyanna is strong about what she believes, and wants to keep her family and island protected; she wants to be treated like an adult.

“She's strong, and firm when she needs to be, and wants the best for her house and the North."

And talking about her character’s importance, she added: "It took me a while to realize it. I actually woke up one morning, and the first thing I thought was, 'Wow, I really am integral to the story line.'"

When did The Last Of Us premiere?

Bella plays Ellie in HBO's drama series, The Last Of Us.

HBO and game developer, Naughty Dog, revealed on March 5, 2020, that The Last Of Us was coming to the small screen.

The highly-anticipated show officially premiered on January 15, 2023.
