Daniel Dae Kim is Latest Celebrity Busted For DUI

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, February 9, 2024

Daniel Dae Kim, who stars as Jin-Soo Kwon on Lost, was nabbed by the Honolulu police department Thursday morning on suspicion of drunken driving.

According to the Honolulu Advertiser, Kim was arrested around 3:00 a.m., and at the time was wearing a polo shirt and khaki shorts. Kim was released about two hours later, seen in video trying to hide his face as he left. Details of the arrest of Daniel Dae Kim were not immediately available, but his mug shot is…

Daniel Dae Kim: Giving Riley Giles a run for his mug shot glory.

Daniel Dae Kim is now the fourth member of the cast of Lost to be arrested. Michelle Rodriguez and Cynthia Watros were arrested for DUI in December 2005, when police spotted their vehicles weaving along a local road.

Both pleaded guilty to the charges, and were then “lost” from the show soon after. Man, that was a creative one on the part of The Hollywood Gossip.

Then, in September of 2006, Adewale Akinnuoye-Agbaje was arrested and accused of disobeying a police officer and driving without a license.

Those charges were later dropped… but he was still dropped from the show as his character was killed off. Then again, so are many characters.
