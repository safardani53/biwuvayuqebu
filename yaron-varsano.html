Yaron Varsano (born on 23 June 1975) is a renowned Israeli business tycoon and real estate developer of Dutch descent.

Who is Yaron Varsano?

Yaron Varsano is a business tycoon and Israeli real estate developer of Dutch descent. He is married to actress Gal Gadot, who played the title character in the superhero smash hit “Wonder Woman” in 2017.

Versano was raised in Amsterdam with his brother and then completed his schooling in New York before relocating to Tel Aviv.

He co-founded a real estate company with his brother as a talented entrepreneur.

Over time, they turned it into one of the industry’s most lucrative businesses.

The brothers formerly ran a five-star hotel in Tel Aviv with the same name. They made a significant profit when they sold the Versano Hotel to Russian billionaire Roman Abramovich in 2015.

They both have high expectations for their careers and are supportive of one another’s efforts. The couple splits their time between Los Angeles and Tel Aviv.

Early Life of Yaron Varsano

Yaron Varsano was born on 23 June 1975 in Amsterdam, Netherlands to Jewish parents.

Talking about his parents, his father is Daniel Varsano and his mother is Irit Varsano. He also has a brother as his sibling named Guy Varsano.

He and his brother Guy lived in Amsterdam for a sizable portion of their lives.

Varsano attended the International School of Amsterdam. Later he pursued his further education in Old Westbury, New York at the New York Institute of Technology, where he earned his diploma in 2000.

Yaron Varsano Age

Yaron Varsano Career

Yaron Versano moved to Tel Aviv, Israel after completing his education and founded a real estate company there with his brother.

With his ambition and determination, he quickly made the business lucrative.

They frequently found themselves staying in hotels as they traveled back and forth between Israel and the US.

Following the filming of “Fast & Furious,” the fourth installment in the Fast and the Furious franchise, was released in 2009.

During this time, they happened upon a hotel that was constructed to look like an apartment building due to their desperate urge to be at home.

Russian business mogul Roman Abramovich reportedly paid NIS 100 million (close to $26 million) to buy the hotel in 2015.

Yaron Varsano Wife

Yaron Varsano is married to his wife Gal Gadot. He first met Gadot in 2006 at a yoga retreat that a friend of theirs had organized in the Israeli desert. They were drawn to one another right away.

He told her he was serious about their relationship and wouldn’t wait more than two years to propose to her on their second date.

Varsano, who is ten years older than Gadot, understood exactly what he wanted out of life, whereas Gadot, who was in her early twenties at the time, subsequently acknowledged that she was a little unsure.

In actuality, Gadot was expecting Maya at the time “Wonder Woman” was being filmed.
