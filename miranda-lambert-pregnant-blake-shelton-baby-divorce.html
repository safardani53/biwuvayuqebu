Blake Shelton And Ex Expecting A Baby After Split? Hollywood Life

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, January 28, 2024


Rumors have been swirling about what went wrong ever since Blake and Miranda announced their split. Now, a new repot claims that the pair will be welcoming a baby in the upcoming months. “Miranda is pregnant,” an insider told In Touch. Lo and behold she reportedly didn’t find out until after the pair split, according to the outlet. “Blake and Miranda were in the midst of one of their many screaming matches at their home in Nashville in early July.”

 

The source continued to say, “She’d just read him the riot act over his drinking and flirting, and he retaliated with claims that she’d taken all the fun out of his life and always seemed to resist talk of starting a family.” It was then that Miranda reportedly told him that she refused to have kids “until he got his act together.” Click to hear more details about the Miranda and Blake split.

A few days later, Miranda reportedly learned she was expecting. “She reasoned that [the pregnancy] happened during an increasingly rare night of makeup sex,” says the source. Wow, talk about a bombshell. It’s hard to believe that Blake would go through with the divorce if he and Miranda were expecting.

HollywoodLife.com has reached out to Miranda’s rep for comment.

Miranda & Blake’s Baby Battle Caused A Riff

As HollywoodLife.com previously reported it was the blonde beauty who wanted children. “Miranda felt very lonely in their marriage and really wanted a baby,” a source told HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY. “She really thought having a child together would solve a lot of their issues. Unfortunately, having a baby wasn’t a priority for Blake who was focused on his career and having fun. He was enjoying his life and wasn’t ready to make that kind of commitment. The baby battle really put a strain on their relationship.”
