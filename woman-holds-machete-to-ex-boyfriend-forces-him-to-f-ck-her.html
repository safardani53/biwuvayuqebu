Woman Holds Machete to Ex-Boyfriend, Forces Him to F-ck Her

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 3, 2024

A 19-year old woman in Montana was arrested late last week and charged with aggravated battery and assault with a weapon.

This is disturbing and dangerous on its own.

But the story has gone viral for the unusual reason why she was charged with aggravated battery and assault with a weapon.

According to the Great Falls Tribune, Samantha Ray Mears was taken into custody on Friday after she broke into an ex-boyfriend’s home while he was away.

But the ex (of seven years) eventually returned, only to be confronted by Mears and the large knife in her hand.

Mears ordered this unnamed and unlucky young man to take off all of clothes and lie on his bed.

From there, with the machete at her ex-lover’s face, Mears took off her pants and climbed on top of the frightened guy.

Mears then began to engage him in sexual intercourse, all while still holding the machete.

When he tried to stop the pounding, Mears bit him on the harm.

After the sexual deed was done, Mears sat nude on the bed, still brandishing the weapon, it must be noted.

At that point, the victim managed to snap several photos of her, which he later turned over to the police as evidence.

Before the authorities got involved, however, an argument ensued between the exes, prompting an angry Mears to rip a piece of trim from the victim’s wall and deliberately urinated in his bed.

The ex-boyfriend somehow conviced Mears shortly afterward that he needed to call a friend, escaping from the room to dial 911.

According to the Tribune, Mears was also arrested in April after a previous fight at the victim’s home where she grabbed his hair, hit him in the face and attempted to strangle him.
