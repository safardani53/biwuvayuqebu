Phillip Anthony Beasley, known by his stage name Philthy Rich is an American Rapper who owns SCMMLLC and is distributed by Empire Distribution. He started his career in 2007 when he recruited fellow rappers Mistah F.A.B., J. Stalin, Shady Nate, Beeda Weeda, Ray Ryda, Eddi Projex and Lil Al tha Gamer to join him on tour. The tour, "Stan Bizzness BG's", was canceled after Philthy was arrested for selling cocaine and marijuana, after which he was sentenced to one year in prison. He signed with RBC Records & eOne Music in 2013 and released N.E.R.N.L. June 18. The album features guest appearances by Fabolous, Gunplay and Dc Young Fly Problem French Montana Trae tha Truth, among others.
