Amber Rose & Wiz Khalifa Back On? She Would Dump MGK For Him

While Amber wants Wiz back, she’s giving all her attention to other men. First, the blonde beauty got naked in bed with her male co-star for her new flick Sister Code, then she started a romance with a different rapper, Machine Gun Kelly, aka MGK. Obvi, Miss Rose is doing her best to show Wiz what he’s missing.

“[Amber] likes MGK, but he doesn’t hold a candle to Wiz,” a source tells HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY. “She’s just having fun, but also sending Wiz the message that she’s a bad b***h who can pull any dude she wants.”

But it’s all just a front. Amber desperately wants to become Wiz’s one and only once more.

“Amber wants Wiz. Loves him. Can’t live without him,” the insider adds. “But Amber’s like a deer and she wants to be hunted. She loves the chase and she wants Wiz to come after her hard. If Wiz chased her more, Amber would dump Machine Gun Kelly in a heartbeat to be with him again.”

Is Wiz willing to work for it, though?

Wiz Khalifa Reveals He Wants Amber Rose Back In New Song

It’s pretty apparent that Wiz dreams about making it work again with Amber one day. A new track, “Somebody Else” hit the web on April 27 — and the rapper spits some telling rhymes about his relationship with the mother of his son, Sebastian.

Check out his striking lyrics: “An empty hole in my heart/ Will I ever love again?/ You had your way and you know it/ I’ve spent a lot of time in the past/ Thinkin’ bout how we could have lasted,” Wiz says in the new tune.
