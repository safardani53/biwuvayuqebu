Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, February 13, 2024

Sienna Miller Topless On ‘Esquire’:

Sienna rose to fame in 2006 for her role in the biopic Factory Girl. Soon after, the actress took a three year hiatus following her very public breakup with ex, Jude Law. Sienna attributed her early success in-part to her bad behavior, “I think I’d been lucky in that I’d always been naughty in that town and people had always liked me for it. (But) I sabotaged things. I burnt a lot of bridges. I never read a review or paid any mind to what anyone said.” She went on to explain that at work she was different from the person that the public saw. “On set, I was first to arrive, last to leave, best friends with the crew, totally professional, no d***ing around.”

 Sienna’s Happy Life With Marlowe And Tom

In recent years it looks like Sienna has left the past right where it belongs, in the past! She and Tom welcomed beautiful baby girl, Marlowe, in 2012 — and she loves being a mom. “I feel really settled,” she said. “I have a wonderful life. I’m up every morning with a little baby. That gives you incredible perspective. It’s such a gift. And it’s grounding in a way you can’t describe. And going back to work is really exciting and fun.”

Sienna has also been extremely happy in the love department, with fiance Tom. “He’s the antithesis of me, really. And that’s a first for me, to be in a relationship with someone who’s in a lot of respects very different,” she said.
