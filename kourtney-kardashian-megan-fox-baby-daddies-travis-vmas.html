Kourtney Kardashian and Megan Fox introduce 'future baby daddies' Travis Barker &amp; Machine Gun Kelly

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

KOURTNEY Kardashian and Megan Fox introduced their “future baby daddies” Travis Barker & Machine Gun Kelly at the 2021 MTV VMAs. 

The brunette bombshells presented the last performance of the night as their boyfriends took the Barclays Center stage in New York City to rock out to their new song, A Girl Like You. 

Megan, 35, rocked a complete see-through dress with hair that appeared wet while Kourtney, 42, wore a black leather corset dress. 

The Transformers star took the mic first, and teased the upcoming act: “I’m a huge fan of this next performer. I’ve watched him grow and not just as an artist but also as a person.”

“I’m a fan too and I think his drummer is super hot,” her new friend Kourtney said of her man. 

“New York I need you to get loud for our future baby daddies,” Megan said before the KUWTK star added their names, “Machine Gun Kelly and Travis Barker.”

The duo then performed their latest single, with MGK providing vocals and Travis doing what he does best on the drum set.

Both Megan and Kourtney began dating their rocker boyfriends earlier this year and have publicly gushed of their new romances in the months since.

The reality star wore a sexy black leather corset dress while the rock legend was shirtless underneath the matching blazer.

More red carpet arrivals include Call Me By Your Name rapper Lil Nas X - who donned a purple off-the-shoulder jumpsuit that had a large dress overcoat complete with a long train. 

As the 22-year-old arrived for the VMAs red carpet at the Brooklyn, New York venue - where the show is held for the first time since 2013 - he was surrounded by a helpful entourage.
