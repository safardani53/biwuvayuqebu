Kourtney Kardashian Young: Photos Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, April 7, 2024

Kourtney Kardashian has grown up in front of the camera & you can see photos of her over the years here.

She may have started as the quiet Kardashian sister but she has since become a force to be reckoned with. Over the years, Kourtney has grown from a mere reality star into a cultural icon, fashionista, and mother-of-three.

The eldest Kardashian sister rose to prominence along with her family in 2007, with the premiere of ‘Keeping Up With The Kardashians’. From there, the projects multiplied, with spin-offs like including ‘Kourtney and Khloé Take Miami’ and ‘Kourtney and Kim Take New York’.  She enjoyed clothing collaborations with brands like Bebe and PrettyLittleThing. In Mar. 2019, she founded the lifestyle site Poosh.

Through it all, Kourtney’s main focus has been motherhood and family. The star shares three children, Mason, Penelope, and Reign, with ex Scott Disick. She has been married to rocker Travis Barker since 2022 and the two are expecting a child.
