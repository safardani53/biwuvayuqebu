Thongchai Jaidee (Golfer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, April 8, 2024

Thongchai Jaidee is a professional Thai golfer participating in Asian and European tournaments. On the Asian tour, he holds the record for most money earned in his career and is second for most wins, winning 13 times. He has won the Certificate of Merit three times during his career Asian tour. Jaidee was the first man to win the cash prize of $ 2 million, $ 3 million, $ 4 million and $ 5 million, respectively, on the Asian tour. Jaidee didn't start playing golf until he was 16, then joined the Royal Thai Army, where he was a paratrooper in the Special Forces. Jaidee didn't turn pro until he was thirty, but he quickly rose to success on the Asian Tour, topping the league's list of achievements in 2001 and 2004. His first major league appearance of the US Open in 2001 and finished in 74th place. In February 2004, he became the first Thai to win the European Tour by winning the Carlsberg Malaysia Open, an event co-hosted with the Asian Tour.
