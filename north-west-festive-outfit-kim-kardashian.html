North West, 10, reveals her festive outfit in new video taken inside huge bedroom at mom Kim Kardash

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, May 7, 2024

NORTH West has been seen in a new TikTok video showing off her incredible holiday spirit.

The ten-year-old posted the video on the TikTok account she shares with her mom Kim Kardashian, 43.

North shot the video in her mom's Los Angeles mansion while surrounded by one pink and two white Christmas trees.

She was wearing an over-the-top Christmas outfit that started with a long red sweater covered in holiday patterns and a large Santa.

Around the bottom of the sweater was a line of sparkly red and green garland.

On the bottom, she was wearing a pair of red and green plaid pajama bottoms and red and white plaid socks.

Her hair was pulled back into pigtails and covered in perfectly curled red and green ribbon.

The Kardashian kid danced before lipsyncing to a sound that said: "Thanksgiving is over and Christmas is on its way."

CRAZY CHRISTMAS

North recently flaunted her over-the-top festive bedroom including its nine Christmas trees and piles of gifts.

The holiday video showed her room, appropriately titled "North Pole" and captured all of its "Holiday decor."

A pink-themed Christmas tree was decorated with all pink embellishments, including pink ornaments, pink bulbs, and pink ribbon.

There were several Christmas presents, wrapped in all-white gift paper, placed underneath the tree.

In addition to the pink tree was a smaller white, silver, and gold-decorated tree.

Another two large Christmas trees were raised on both sides of North's bed and decorated with traditional holiday colors.

On the bed, Santa and elf-shaped pillows were thrown near the head while a plaid blanket was laid on top.

ANOTHER AGENDA

In a previous episode of The Kardashians, Kim brought North to New York to watch her prepare for the Met Gala.

While hanging out in a hotel room during episode nine of the Hulu original series, North hoped she would be invited one day.

"I'd love to be honored at the Met Gala... 2024," she said.

Instead of walking up the famous Met stairs, North hung out with Kim's team and her cousin, Penelope Disick, 11.

They had a party of their own while watching the live streams and being brutally honest about guests' outfits.
