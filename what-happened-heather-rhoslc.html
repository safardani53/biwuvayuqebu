What happened to Heather on RHOSLC?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, May 2, 2024

THE Real Housewives of Salt Lake City is back on our screens – and one lady has been making all the headlines.

Heather Gay shocked fans when she returned to the reality TV series for its third season– but what happened?

Who is Heather on Real Housewives of Salt Lake City?

Born on June 29, 1982, Heather Gay is well known for being a star on Real House Wives of Salt Lake City (RHOSLC).

Aside from her role on RHOSLC, she has a successful medspa called Beauty Lab + Laser.

Her medspa touts itself as a cosmetic medical practice that offers the best lip injections, skin care treatments, and weight loss treatments.

Heather co-founded Beauty + Laser with her business partner, Andrea Robinson.

Read more on Real Housewives

According to Bravo TV, the star was brought up in a devout Mormon household and was married for 11 years.

But she has since distances herself from the church following a divorce and is a self-proclaimed "good Mormon gone bad.”

She has starred in all three seasons of the hit show to date and believes she is always at the centre of the drama.

“I’ve watched Housewives from the beginning, and I feel like we have been in turbo years,” she told In the Know, ahead of season 3's release in September, 2022.

“We have had sonic years, where every year is like 10 years of drama.

“The drama we’ve experienced, the shifts in cast, the issues. … it’s Season 3, but we’re seasoned Housewives.”

What happened to Heather?

Fans have been on tenterhooks since it was revealed that Heather was carrying a black eye.  

Throughout season three, the real housewife has been locked in a feud with Whitney Rose, and some fans believe her injury has something to do with their ongoing issues.

To date, viewers have noticed that relations haven't been the same between the cousins after the cast's Arizona trip.

At the time, Whitney claimed Heather knew about a rumor involving Lisa Barlow using sexual favors to get basketball tickets.

But Heather denied she knew anything about the rumor.

What has Heather said about her black eye?

Creators Bravo have been teasing Heather Gay’s black eye plot since the RHOSLC mid-season trailer was released in November, 2022.

And in season 3 episode 11, viewers saw Heather’s brief exchange with Meredith Marks – hoping that all would be revealed.

In a short segment towards the end of episode, Heather was texting Meredith to come to her room.

And as the latter entered, she saw Jen Shah looking worried, while Heather was sitting with black sunglasses on.

When Meredith asked what happened, Heather removed the sunglasses, revealing a black eye and some marks on her arm.

But crucially, she stopped short of revealing what had happened as the episode concluded.

Since the clip was aired, some fans still believe Heather was attacked, while others have theorised that she might have hurt herself in an accident while intoxicated.

“I would bet that Heathers black eye is from a fall down the stairs when she was drunk and Bravo is trying to play it up like Whitney decked her”, one viewer wrote on Twitter.
