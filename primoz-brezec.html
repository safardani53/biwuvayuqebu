Career

In 2011, he joined Lokomotiv Kuban, and in 2012, he joined Nizhny Novgorod. He played for two clubs in 2015, AEK Larnaca and AI Kuwait. He has been a member of AEK Larnaca in the Cypriot League since 2015 and is currently active with the squad. Primoz announced his retirement from professional basketball on September 2, 2017, and joined the Cleveland Cavaliers as an international scout.
