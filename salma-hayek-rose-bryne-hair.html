A Big Bump &amp; Side Swept Bangs Get The Look! Hollywood Life

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, April 18, 2024

Harry did Rose’s hair for the May 25 X-Men: First Class movie premiere. The inspiration for her look? Rose herself!

“Rose is such a classic beauty,” said Harry. “I wanted to create a look that complemented her natural sophistication by creating a no-frills up do that showcased her beauty. Her style is so elegant and well put-together yet always appears effortless. I wanted her hair to reflect that – naturally polished and beautiful.”

Follow these simple steps to try the style yourself!

  • Harry started with the new John Frieda Root Lift Foam (a weightless, root-boosting formula that lifts the hair while protecting it from heat styling) while Rose’s hair was wet to give her the lift she needed for the updo.
  • Harry began blowing out her hair with a boar bristle brush, one section at a time, for sleek, straight locks.
