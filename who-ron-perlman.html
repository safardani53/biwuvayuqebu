Who is Ron Perlman? | The US Sun

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, May 15, 2024

RON Perlman rose to fame in the 1990s after starring in the popular Beauty and the Beast TV series.

Since then, he has been seen in over 250 additional projects and has no plans on stopping anytime soon.

Who is Ron Perlman?

Ronald "Ron" Perlman, 72, is an American actor who was born on April 13, 1950, in Washington Heights.

He is famously known for his roles as Vincent in the Beauty and the Beast TV series, as well as for portraying Hellboy in the Hellboy franchise.

Over the years, Perlman has also been seen and heard in Hand of God, Sons of Anarchy, Don't Look Up, American Dad, StartUp, Transformers: Power of the Primes, Adventure Time, and Danny Phantom.

When Perlman is not on set, he can often be found posting about his life and political views on Instagram, where he boasts over 942k followers.

Is Ron Perlman married?

Over the years, Perlman has tied the knot twice.

From 1981 to 2019, he was married to Opal Perlman, who is known as a jewelry designer.

She is also the mother of Perlman's children, Blake and Brandon.

After 38 years of marriage, the two parted ways, and Perlman was ordered by the courts to pay his ex-wife $12,500 a month in perpetuity, US Weekly reports.

Shortly after his divorce, Perlman moved on with his StartUp costar Allison Dunbar, 49.

The two first made headlines in May 2019 after they were spotted kissing in Pasadena, California.

They then tied the knot in 2022.

“Pulling a Kravis before it was a thing,” Dunbar captioned a post, referencing Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker's recent ceremony.

A source exclusively told The Sun the super-private wedding took place at Francis Ford Coppola's property - Palazzo Margherita - in Bernalda, a small town in Southern Italy.
