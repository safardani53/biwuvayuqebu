Why is Tory Lanez hair trending?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, March 20, 2024

PHOTOS of Tory Lanez' went viral on Wednesday after photos surfaced of his patchy hair.

Twitter users took it as an opportunity to mock the rapper.

Why is Tory Lanez trending?

People on social media started sharing photos and videos of Lanez playing basketball.

The aerial shots show bald patches on the top of his head. Twitter of course started sharing memes immediately.

What were people saying on Twitter?

One user said "Tory Lanez’s hair looking like Jamie Fox’s hair in The Amazing Spider-Man 2."

Another joked, "tory lanez wanted to talk all that sh-- about meg, but his hair looking like the water bandit from home alone."

One Twitter user said "Gonna show my son this pic of Tory Lanez and tell him this is what happens to the hair of little boys who lie."

A lot of people on Twitter were referencing the controversy surrounding the alleged shooting of Megan Thee Stallion in July 2020 - something the rapper has denied doing.

Lanez has not appeared to comment on the memes making the rounds on Twitter.

However, on Wednesday, Lanez tweeted with no context, "Lol I know what I be doing."

He also retweeted a Twitter user saying his hairline "looked fresh" and not to worry.

What did Tory Lanez allegedly do to Megan Thee Stallion?

Megan Thee Stallion claimed she was shot in the foot during a shooting outside a Hollywood Hills home in July 2020.

Video footage showed cops demanding the occupants exit the vehicle, with Megan - in a bikini - leaving with her hands in the air and calling out: "My hands are up."
