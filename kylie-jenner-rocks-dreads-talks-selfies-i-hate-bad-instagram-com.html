I Hate Bad Instagram Comments!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, July 15, 2024

Kylie Jenner covers the newest issue of Teen Vogue, and inside the mag, she discusses some of her favorite topics. And by "favorite" we mean the same two or three things she talks about in every interview.

Kylie’s Instagram obsession is well-documented, and the girl considers herself an artist with a makeup brush.

Aside from her relationship with Tyga (which is obviously not Teen Vogue-appropriate), social media and cosmetics are Kylie’s main interests, so she sticks to what she knows in what is essentially the same interview she’s given dozens of times before:

On Family: “Having sisters is the best. I have a different relationship with each of them. I go to Kim for fashion advice, Khloe is always boy and family advice, and Kourtney is like another mother to me.

On Makeup: “I never really wear makeup unless I need to, because when I do my makeup I like to really do my makeup. So I’m never going to spend just 20 minutes, you know? A good night out is, like, two and a half hours for full makeup, curling hair, whatever.

On Instagram: “Today I posted my Instagram picture three different times. I kept deleting it and re-posting it because I kept looking at the comments and they were bad comments.

And I was like, ‘Ahhh!’ and I kept re-posting it. The people who take the time to write these comments are so mean and hateful, like they just want to come at you and hurt you. You can read 1,000 comments and only remember the mean ones. I think you should only listen to what your family and friends say."

Kylie’s "no makeup" selfies are generally just selfies in which she wears slightly less makeup than she usually would, which is still a whole freakin’ lot.

Her comments about Instagram are interesting (we all know how one bad comment can ruin your day), but also sort of BS. 

If Kylie really believed we should all just listen to our family and friends, she wouldn’t need to constantly upload selfies. Then again, that philosophy might explain why she’s slowly morphing into Kim.
