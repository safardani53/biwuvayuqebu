Jaime King, Jessica Alba Wear Christmas Pajamas With Husbands: Picture

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, April 15, 2024

Because, why not? Jaime King and her famous pal Jessica Alba went all out for Christmas with their significant others. As the two actresses celebrated the holiday together, they both posed for a hilarious photo wearing some very wacky pajamas.

“Merry Christmas from the nerds in pj’s! Thanks to @jessicaalba & @cash_warren for the AWESOME digs! And at @kyle_newman holding pose,” 34-year-old King wrote via Instagram on Christmas Day. Alongside the snapshot, both couples posed with one leg in the air. The Hart of Dixie star wore a Mrs. Santa Claus outfit, while her beau Kyle Newman wore a penguin ensemble with a matching Santa hat.

But the four weren’t the only ones to get dolled up! King also posted a sweet picture of her two-month-old son James Knight dressed as her Santa mini-me. “We hope all of you had a Merry Christmas! This little Santa is totally wiped from all the chimney…” she joked.

In the photo, the two women both cuddled James while wearing matching holiday ensembles.
