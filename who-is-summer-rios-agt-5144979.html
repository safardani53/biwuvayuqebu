Who Is Summer Rios? Get To Know The 19-Year-Old Singer On AGT Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 10, 2024
  • Summer auditioned during the August 1 episode of AGT.
  • Summer is a singer.
  • Summer has nearly 500,000 followers on TikTok.

Summer Rios dazzled the America’s Got Talent crowd with her amazing voice during her August 1 audition. Her performance of “Something in the Orange” by Zach Bryan totally impressed the judges. “Your voice is spectacular,” Sofia Vergara raved. Simon Cowell praised her “great choice of song” before all the judges voted to send her to the next round. Now she’s made it to the qualifiers and hopes to make it to the season 18 finale.

Who is Summer Rios? If you’ve watched any of Summer’s past performances, you know this girl has some serious talent. HollywoodLife has rounded up everything you need to know about this 19-year-old singer.

Summer has released original music.

Summer dropped the single “Fingers Crossed” prior to her AGT audition. She’s currently working on a new song titled “The Long Way.” During her audition, she revealed that she’d been singing since she was 4 years old, but she’d never really performed before. She’s trying to change that with AGT.

Summer currently works at Pizza Hut.

She is a manager at Pizza Hut. Summer has frequently posted TikTok videos she filmed at Pizza Hut. “Help me blow up so I can quit my job,” she wrote across one of her videos. She captioned the video, “Don’t get me wrong, I love Pizza Hut… just rather be famous already.”

Summer has a big following on TikTok.

Summer already has 500,000 followers on TikTok. Her videos have earned over 9.8 million likes. She’s been teasing her AGT appearance in several TikTok videos ahead of her audition airing in the August 1 episode.

Summer took a break from music.

Summer revealed in an interview with The Hype Magazine that she took a “year-long break from producing music” to work on her mental health. “It was a life changer for me and gave me more motivation to continue on with music. Nothing will happen right away and that is the reason you should keep pushing. Music is a huge thing for this world and we need more talented artists,” she said.

Summer grew up in Ohio.

Summer is an Ohio native. She grew up in Brunswick, Ohio. In her interview with The Hype Magazine, Summer said that “Machine Gun Kelly has been a big inspiration from Ohio because of his lyrics he has came [sic] up with lately.”
