Dallas Clayton Biography

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, March 18, 2024

As Dallas Clayton was interested in writing since his childhood, he pursued his career in the writing field. At first, Clayton started his profession by self-publishing small run short zines which he distributes in street corners, music venues, and art galleries throughout the United States. However he rose to fame after he published “An Awesome Book!”, which is a 64-page children’s picture book on the subject of “dreaming big”, sold over 50,000 copies to the date. 

Further, he started working as the editorial-in-chief of “We Love You So”, an art magazine founded by an American renowned director Spike Jonze.

In he came out with his second book “An Awesome Book of Thanks!” which followed the pattern of his previous book “An Awesome Book!” which was about the importance of being thankful, released through AmazoneEncore.

As per the source, Clayton had signed a three-book deal with Harper Collins and a two-book deal with Candlewick Press.

Additionally, he released a collection of children’s poetry “Make Magic! Do Good!” as well as children’s book for adults, “It’s Never Too Late” via P. Putnam’s Sons. Throughout his writing career, he has published several books including “A is for Awesome”, “Lily the Unicorn”, and ”Awesome Book of Love! (Harper)”.

As of now, Dallas Clayton might be single and unmarried but he is a father of his son Audio Science Clayton whom he welcomed with his former girlfriend Shannyn Sossamon. The ex-couple almost dated for two years, however, the two got separated after the birth of their son in 2003. Since then, there is no news and rumors related to his affair and girlfriend.

Being a renowned author Dallas Clayton has an accumulated net worth of $3 million. He has earned his wealth widely through his career as an author.
