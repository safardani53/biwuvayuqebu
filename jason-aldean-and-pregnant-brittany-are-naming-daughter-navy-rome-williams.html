Jason Aldean and Pregnant Brittany Are Naming Daughter Navy Rome Williams

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, February 15, 2024

Bring on a blue nursery! Jason Aldean and his wife, Brittany Kerr, will welcome a baby girl named Navy Rome Williams in February. The pregnant American Idol alum, 30, and Aldean’s 11-year-old daughter Kendyl announced the colorful moniker on Sunday, November 4.

“It’s Brit and Kendyl here,” Kerr began in a clip posted on her Instagram Story. “And we are so excited for . . .”

“Navy Rome Williams!” exclaimed a beaming Kendyl. (Kendyl and Keeley, 15, are Aldean’s children from his previous marriage to Jessica Ussery.) The country music star, 41, and the makeup artist, who tied the knot in March 2015, are also parents of 11-month-old son Memphis. 

The sweet video was filmed at Kerr’s Sunday baby shower at Allenbrooke Farms in Spring Hill, Tennessee. Four-time Grammy nominee Aldean made an appearance at the party, where he piled his plate with Southern cuisine including biscuits. “Happy baby shower day,” Kerr cracked on Instagram Story. “Looks like you found what you came for, cause you always eating.” Guests also feasted on sprinkled doughnuts and cupcakes.

While Aldean was casual in jeans, Kerr showed off her baby bump in a skintight bodycon dress.

The “You Make It Easy” singer and Kerr shocked fans in July when they revealed they are expecting a baby girl just seven months after welcoming Memphis. 

“I’m about to have two under the age of a year and a half, so it’s exciting,” Aldean told Us Weekly and other reporters in September. The Georgia native isn’t worried about having back-to-back babies. “We’re excited to have them close together in age,” he explained at the time. “I think it’s going to be fun and hopefully they’’ll grow up really close and have a built-in best friend. That’s kind of what we’re hoping for.”
