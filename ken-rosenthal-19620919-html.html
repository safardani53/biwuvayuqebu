After working as a sports intern for Newsday on Long Island, he began his career in 1984 at the York Daily Record. He moved to the Courier-Post in Cherry Hill, New Jersey for two years before securing a full-time job with The Baltimore Sun, where he was named Maryland Sports Writer of the Year by the Sports Writers and Sports Association. . . from 1987 to 2000. Rosenthal also contributed to Sports Illustrated from 1990 to 2000, providing weekly ratings during the baseball season. He then spent 5 years at The Sporting News before joining Fox Sports in 2005
