Brad Pitt denies dating jewellery designer and spiritual guru Sat Hari Khalsa The Sun

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, March 28, 2024

BRAD Pitt isn't dating jewellery designer Sat Hari Khalsa - after rumours circulated he had turned to the spiritual guru after his split from Charlize Theron.

A source close to the Ad Astra star, 55, told The Sun Online the reports were "totally false".

It's not the first time the pair have been linked.

Last year tongues wagged after they attended a charity art auction.

The actor was pictured sitting next to the designer and holistic therapist at Silverlake Conservatory of Music annual benefit and art auction, with The Hollywood Reporter claiming they attended the event "together".

The pair were spotted laughing and talking at the charity party, leaving US websites convinced Brad was dating Sat.

Sat is well known in Hollywood – Brad’s ex wife Jennifer Aniston has worn her jewellery designs and she’s toured with the Red Hot Chili Peppers as their "holistic nurse".

However, insiders close to Brad told The Sun Online that they were not dating and are “simply friends”.

“They did not attend together,” the insider added.

The gala was hosted by Flea from the Red Hot Chili Peppers, who is close friends with Brad – which is possibly how he knows Sat.

Brad has been linked to several women since his split with Angelina three years ago.

Last year, US reports claimed he was dating MIT professor Neri Oxman, and prior to that, The Sun revealed he was dating Sienna Miller after they were spotted cuddling up at Glastonbury.

But after the relationship fizzled out, The Sun’s Bizarre column revealed Brad was on a year-long dating hiatus and was planning to stay celibate.

His latest romance was with A-lister Charlize Theron after the pair grew close while filming a Breitling watch ad. However, they split in June.
