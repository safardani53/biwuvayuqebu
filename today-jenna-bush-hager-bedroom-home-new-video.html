Todays Jenna Bush Hager shares new video from bed as she reveals rare look inside her 'beautiful

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, October 7, 2024

JENNA Bush Hager has given her Today fans an inside look at her home in a series of new videos.

The host showed off one of her bedrooms and explained what she thinks makes the space look “beautiful.”

Though Jenna, 41, mostly shares photos from her time on the morning show or from travels with her family, she offered followers a rare glimpse at her home life on Thursday.

Busy with on-air gigs, a book club, and her production company, Jenna admitted her “time in bed is so precious.”

She sat on the plush bedding in one of her rooms in blue pants and a half-sleeve, button-up blouse as she dished on her sponsored partnership with the brand Boll & Branch.

“My alarm clock goes off way, way too early,” Jenna said in the Instagram video. “So, my time in bed is a commodity.”

The Today host said she enjoys using their sheets because “they are so soft,” which is why they are used in all of the bedrooms in her home.

While Jenna revealed she had the same brand in her kids’ rooms, guest room, and her own bedroom, she did not reveal which of the spaces she was in for the video.

Aside from the textured white sheets and pillowcases, the clips also gave a look at the gorgeous pale yellow and blue floral headboard.

Gray curtains were closed behind the furniture, while a small wooden nightstand could be seen on the side of the bed.

She shared: “When friends come to stay, they are always so impressed with how soft the sheets are and how pretty the beds look.”

"Their duvets are so soft, it feels like you're sleeping on a cloud," the mother-of-three added, before giving fans a discount code as part of her promotion with the company.

Aside from revealing a look inside her home, Jenna also got a bit revealing when it came to her personal life this week.

During an episode of their Today series, Jenna and Hoda Kotb agreed on one of the most "humiliating" parts of growing up.

The pair got on the topic of buying their first bras, and Hoda said: "The most awful experience known to man…woman. It is."

She continued: "Because they used to be called training bras, which was always humiliating."

"It felt like you were wearing diapers," Jenna agreed.
