Fritz Balogh Professional Life

Balogh began playing football as a 13-year-old in 1933 when he founded the German Sports Club Bratislava. He was promoted to the first team for the 1939/40 season. During the Second World War, he ended up in Berlin. He first started for Hertha BSC’s third team, but was quickly “promoted” to the first. He was a member of the Berlin team.
