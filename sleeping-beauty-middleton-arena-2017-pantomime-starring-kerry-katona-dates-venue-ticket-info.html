When is Sleeping Beauty at Middleton Arena? 2017 pantomime starring Kerry Katona - dates, venue and

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 1, 2024

GET ready to say “he’s behind you” and “oh yes he is” as pantomime season is around the corner.

But when is Sleeping Beauty being held in Manchester and how can you get tickets for the whole family?

When is Sleeping Beauty at Middleton Arena?

Manchester will soon play host to the pantomime classic Sleeping Beauty, with a number of famous faces in the mix.

It is being held at Middleton Arena and showings started on Thursday November 30.

Performances will run to Wednesday December 27.

The 500-seat theatre, located just north of the city, will hold family and school showings during the day and in the evening during the festive period.

The website description says: “The evil Carabosse curses princess Beauty to die on her 16th birthday. Thanks to Beauty’s fairy, she only falls into a deep sleep that can be ended with a kiss from her true love. Will her handsome prince come to her rescue and save the day?”

What celebrities are starring in Sleeping Beauty at Middleton Arena?

Atomic Kitten’s Kerry Katona will be starring in the pantomime in the role of the evil Carabosse, so look out for her bringing fierce sass to the stage.

Richard Aucott has been announced as playing the dame, Nanny Nora and Wink Taylor, who starred in CITV’s The Sooty Show is playing the King.

Miles Walker will be playing the Prince and Keri Rae will play the Princess Beauty.

How can you get tickets for Sleeping Beauty at Middleton Arena?

Tickets are available from the Link4Life website or from See Tickets, or call 0300 303 8633

There is currently an opening night offer, with all tickets costing £10.50 for Friday December 1.

The rest of tickets will be priced £16 for adult off-peak, £18 for adult peak, which rises to £18.50 for Christmas Eve.

Children & Concession tickets cost £14 off-peak, £15 peak and £16 for Christmas Eve.

There is also a family ticket available for two adults and two children.

This costs £52 off-peak, £56 peak and £60 for Christmas Eve.
