Meaning Behind His Name

St. Brown’s father, John Brown, a bodybuilding elit and a two-time Mr. Universe, named his son “Amon-Ra Julian Heru J. St. Brown.”

Originating from Egyptian roots, the Lions’ wide receiver, St. Brow,n disclosed that his name, Amon-Ra, is inspired by Amun.

In Egyptian mythology, Amun is celebrated as a deity embodying the essence of air, the force of creation, and the sun’s radiant power.

In the early 1990s, St. Brown’s father actively participated in what he characterized as an “underground black consciousness movement.”

During this period, he discovered the potency of traditional African names, with a particular fascination for Egyptian nomenclature.

Hence, the distinctive name of Amon-Ra is due to John’s exploration of African and Egyptian traditions and reflects their cultural significance.

Furthermore, John named his other two sons “Equanimeous Tristan Imhotep J. St. Brown,” and “Osiris Adrian Amon-Ra J. St. Brown.”
