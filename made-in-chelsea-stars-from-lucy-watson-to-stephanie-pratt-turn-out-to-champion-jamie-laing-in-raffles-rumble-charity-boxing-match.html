Made In Chelsea stars from Lucy Watson to Stephanie Pratt turn out to cheer on Jamie Laing in Raffle

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, June 29, 2024

CAST members old and new from Made In Chelsea were out in force to cheer on Jamie Laing in a charity boxing match on Saturday night.

The blond haired 28-year-old took to the ring to fight Hamish Ross at exclusive Chelsea nightclub Raffles in the Raffles Rumble white collar boxing event.

Proceeds from the night were to be donated to the Miles Frost Fund - which is part of the British Heart Foundation and set up in the wake of the sudden death of Sir David Frost's son Miles of hypertrophic cardiomyopathy aged 31 in 2015.

There was a risk that Jamie and Hamish (believed to be the Operations Director at Raffles & Director at L Venue Ltd) wouldn't be the only ones fighting, however, as the cast mates have a tendency to launch into explosive arguments.

Lottie Moss and Nicola Hughes were two of the beauties attending the charity event - and while both do not currently star in the hit E4 show, they may have a frosty relationship.

Nicola - who starred in the show in the past - was in a relationship with Lottie's current boyfriend (and current MiC castmemeber) Alex Mytton.

Both ladies looked prepared for an enjoyable night - with Nicola dressed in a shining silver coat and Lottie opting for a fluffy coat - however it is believed they steered well clear of each other during the course of the night.

Nicola previously quit Made in Chelsea following an epic fall out with Alex - branding her ex a "gross, vile human."

Fellow former MiC stars Lucy Watson and James Dunmore also arrived to support Jamie in his fight.

Lucy arrived wearing thigh high boots, a cream skirt, dark top and black coat, while James - fresh from a trip to Australia - wore a camel colour jacket and black top.

Current star - and Lucy's on-off friend - Stephanie Pratt looked thrilled to be attending the match.

She shared a selfie with a male friend on social media, writing: "Watching @raffleschelsea boxing charity match with one of my favourites @richardmsquire ðŸ’• always the best company!!!"

However she was also spied chatting with Lucy - suggesting the pair were having an enjoyable evening together.

Other MiC stars included Olivia Bently, Joshua "JP" Patterson, Sam Thompson and Tiffany Watson (and fellow ex-star Fran Newman-Young was also in attendance) all hoping to see Jamie triumph.

Jamie's girlfriend Frankie Graff was ring-side to watch her man fight - wearing a black dress and knee-high boots.

Jamie had taken training for his night in the rings seriously.

"My boi @felixfitness1 has trained me to my peak fitness ready for this Saturday and our white collar boxing match!" he wrote on Instagram earlier in the day - sharing a video of him training.

"He's the best in the business - thanks coach @corecollective," Jamie added.
