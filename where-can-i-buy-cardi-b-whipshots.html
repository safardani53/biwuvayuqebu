Where can I buy Cardi B Whipshots?

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 24, 2024

RAPPER and first ever creative director of Playboy, Cardi B has some new treats up her sleeve.

Fans can now "party like Cardi" according to Cardi B's Whipshot's website.

Where can I buy Cardi B Whipshots?

Cardi has made her way into the spiked dessert world with her new vodka infused whipped cream shots.

The Whipshots are available exclusively on the Whipshots website for all of December 2021 before they hit stores nationwide in early 2022, Buzzfeed reports.

The cans come in three different sizes and prices:

  • $5.99 for 50mL
  • $13.99 for 200mL
  • $19.99 for 375mL

Each can contains 10% alcohol no matter the size you choose.

MORE CARDI B

What flavors do Whipshots come in?

The whip cream shots come in three different flavors, with a different colored can respectively.

There is Mocha, Vanilla, and Caramel to choose from.

The Whipshot website states: "Gold-plate your taste buds with the buttery sweet treat of caramel whipped cream infused with ultra-premium vodka."

The cans are stated to be shelf-stable, meaning they do not require refrigeration and will hold at room temperature.

They also say they are are dairy free and have specific "do not refrigerate" instructions on the back.

When is the next drop for Whipshots?

There will be exclusive daily drops for the rest of December on the Whipshots website before they hit store shelves in early 2022.

The site entices its customers and Cardi B fans with their rich descriptions of the flavors online.

"Stimulate the palate with rich coffee and cocoa notes in decadent whipped cream infused with ultra-premium vodka," the website states.

Cardi wrote in an Instagram post on December 1, 2021 about the Whipshots: "MY WHIPSHOTS ARE LIVE IN 15 HOURS!!!!!

"Time to get drunk and naughty!!!!!"

The new Creative Director of Playboy wrote: "VODKA WHIP CREAM!!!"

On another post on the first day of the drop, the Grammy winner told fans they would be experiencing something totally new, adding: "You never had whipped cream like this before."
