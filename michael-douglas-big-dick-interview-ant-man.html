Michael Douglas Big D**k Actor Raves About It In New Interview Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 22, 2024


Vanity Fair‘s George Wayne asked Michael about his key to superstardom at an “Ant Man” after party. George said, “Your father had his cleft chin. Omar Sharif had the gap in his teeth ...” Michael smiled and responded “I have a big d**k.” So funny! Michael isn’t the only funny guy in the cast of “Ant Man” — Paul Rudd did something hilarious on the set that he recalled to Jimmy Fallon on July 13.

The actor said he tried very hard to bond with Michael while shooting their film, “I thought, ‘I’m really gonna try and be buddy-buddy with the guy,’ and it was his last day. We were doing a close-up, and I thought, you know what I’m gonna do? I’m gonna Basic Instinct him. When he’s speaking, I’m sitting in a chair across from him, and I’m going to, you know.”

His big plan didn’t go exactly as he expected, though. “I should’ve just stopped, and I didn’t, and it looked like I was basically pleasuring myself to Michael Douglas giving a monologue,” Rudd explained. “He just stopped and just looked at me and said, ‘What are you, a f—ing pervert?’ No smiles,” Paul said! Omg — how funny?!
