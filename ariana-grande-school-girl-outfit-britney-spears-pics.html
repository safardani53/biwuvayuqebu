Ariana Grande & Britney Spears Wear School Girl Outfits – Who Wore It Better?

Ariana rocked the 90s look for the Tokyo performance, complete with a plaid skirt and white tights. The singer managed to spice it up by donning a grey crop top, flaunting her toned midriff.

Of course, Ariana added her own signature touch to the throwback look. She wore her hair half-up half-down and wore some sky-high heels. 

Ariana must have drawn inspiration from the one and only Britney! We all remember watching Britney dance up and down the halls for her “…Baby One More Time” video in 1999. (It will forever be a classic.)

In the video, Britney wore a white shirt that she tied into a crop top that revealed her pink bra. She also donned a grey sweater, black tights and girly pigtails.

Honestly, we can’t decide who looks better. Ariana and Britney always look hot. That’s why we are leaving it in your hands!
