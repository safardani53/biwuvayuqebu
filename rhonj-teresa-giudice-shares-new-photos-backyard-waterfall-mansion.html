RHONJ's Teresa Giudice shares stunning new photos of her backyard WATERFALL at $3.4M mansion

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 11, 2024

RHONJ star Teresa Giudice has shared stunning new photos of her backyard waterfall at her $3.4million New Jersey mansion.

The reality star purchased the Montville Township home with her fiancé Luis Ruelas in February 2021.

This isn't the first time The Real Housewives of New Jersey star showed off the beautiful home that she shares with fiance Luis Ruelas.

The reality star posted a boomerang clip a few weeks ago of her large pool with cascading waterfalls over a rock structure.

The pair bought the sprawling estate, which sits at 7,728 square feet and features seven bedrooms and seven full bathrooms.

Teresa and Luis purchased the new home together a few months into their relationship.

HOME SWEET HOME

Over the past couple of months, the Bravo star has shown off the gorgeous landscape surrounding her new multi-million pad.

Teresa's daughter Milania, 16, recently gave fans a glimpse at their full gym inside the family's new mansion.

The two appeared in a TikTok video flexing their muscles.

In the background, the gym had a flat-screen TV, weights, and a black punching bag.

The basement also featured a bed, a bathroom, and a rec room.

The first floor housed the kitchen, dining room, living room, an office with wooden furniture and a large brown leather chair, and a laundry room, along with one bedroom and a full bath.

The second floor came with five more bedrooms, another laundry room, and four more bathrooms.

The backyard included a flower-filled garden with a huge lounge bed for sunbathing.

BLENDED FAMILY

The RHONJ star shares her girls, Gia, 21, Gabriella, 18, Milania, 16, and Audriana, 13, with her ex-husband Joe Giudice.

Her four daughters also live at the luxurious oasis, which boasts plenty of room for the blended family.

Luis' children include two sons, David, who just started college in August 2021, and a younger son who is autistic.
