Same As Naya Rivera VOTE Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 15, 2024

Shay Mitchell, 29, and Naya Rivera, 30, both headed to the same event on Feb. 21st and they landed themselves in a bit of a pickle. They arrived at the Vanity Fair and L’Oreal Paris Toast to Young Hollywood party in LA and they both rocked plunging black outfits. We love both of their looks so much and we can’t decide who wore it better. What do you think?

Shay Mitchell Best Beauty Looks

Shay looked fabulous when she arrived on the purple carpet wearing a black satin suit. She opted to wear an Elizabeth and James Lainey Blazer and Foster Trousers from the Spring 2017 collection and it fit her like a glove. The low-cut blazer showed off a ton of skin and Shay opted to go braless underneath flashing massive cleavage. She paired the top with skinny-leg trousers, black strappy bedazzled pumps, and dangling diamond earrings.

Naya went with something almost identical, but in a dress version. She donned a skin-tight black mini dress with long billowing sleeves and a plunging neckline, which she also opted to go braless under. The neckline of the dress showed off serious cleavage and her legs looked so long and lean in this mini! She topped her look off with simple black ankle-strap sandals, a satin black clutch, and diamond stud earrings.

We are honestly loving the way they both looked in their plunging black outfits and we can’t decide who wore it better! What do you guys think? VOTE.
