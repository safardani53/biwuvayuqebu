Who is Love Island bombshell Lynda Flix?

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 30, 2024

CASA Amor is upon us and a string of sexy singles are set to appear on Love Island.

The new girls and boys include Lynda Flix, who is set to shake up the villa as she tries to find love for herself.

Who is Lynda Flix?

Lynda works as a healthcare assistant and singer and comes from Salford but is swapping that for Love Island.

The 22-year-old is ready to shake things up in Casa Amor as she sets her sights on the original boys.

It seems she's already particularly interested in some boys in particular.

She said: "Kai and Shaq, they’re both tall, dark and handsome.

"Tom too, I like his eyes and they seem to be doing something for the girls, I want to experience that."

She said: "The most important thing is humour, he needs to be funny and be able to make me laugh.

"I also need someone that's very energetic and exciting, he can’t be boring. If he’s boring, Bye!"
