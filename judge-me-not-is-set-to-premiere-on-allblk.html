Judge Me Not Is Set To Premiere On ALLBLK

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, April 21, 2024

Judge Me Not, ALLBLK’s latest drama series is planned to premiere on Thursday, May 25, 2023. The plot revolves on a young lady who is dealing with a number of personal issues. She takes on the position of a judge at a court, but the task is much more difficult than she had anticipated. The show stars Chyna Layne as the protagonist, with a number of others portraying crucial supporting parts. It is presided over by Judge Lynn Toler.

The trailer for ALLBLK’s Judge Me Not promises an intriguing courtroom comedy drama.

On April 28, 2023, ALLBLK released the official trailer for Judge Me Not, which gives a peek at the countless dramatic events that will take place in protagonist Zelma Jay Johnson’s life. The trailer starts off on an amusing and embarrassing note, with Zelma having a nightmare about puking medicines in court. It then depicts various crazy incidents that occur in the courtroom.

Overall, the trailer maintains a hilarious, engaging, and dramatic tone that fans of courtroom dramas and comedy programs will like. According to ALLBLK’s YouTube page, below is the official description of the show:

”Judge Me Not is the narrative of Zelma Jay Johnson, a young black lady with extraordinarily brilliant mental health concerns, a bizarre family, and a turbulent love life. We accompany her as she begins her new career as a judge in a court filled with eccentric personalities both in front of and behind the bench.”

The description continues:

”As her intense connection with her live-in boyfriend takes them both on a wild trip, she finds herself in the crosshairs of a psychological thriller that threatens her life.”

According to the description and trailer, viewers can expect a fascinating and quirky courtroom comedy series that addresses a variety of topics such as mental health, relationships, balancing work and personal life, and many more. The network has not yet specified the number of episodes or the distribution timetable.

More about the cast of Judge Me Not

In Judge Me Not, Chyna Layne portrays Zelma Jay Johnson. Layne is an extraordinarily creative and smart lady who is dealing with several challenges in her love life and with her family, which seem to be affecting her mental health.

She begins working as a judge in a courtroom, where she encounters a number of unexpected problems. Her travel is crucial to the plot, and it will be intriguing to watch how her character is developed. Layne’s other prominent acting roles include to mention a few, She’s Gotta Have It, Daddy’s Home, and Life of Crime. Other notable supporting performers that appear with her include:

  • Martin Allen is played by Jonathan Chase.
  • Barbara Lester is played by Gena Shaw.
  • Daryl Bledsoe is played by Javon Terrell.
  • Michael Johnson is played by Terrence Green.
  • Jean is played by Bonita Brisker.

The program is based on the life of Lynn Tolder, a former renowned judge. Don’t miss Judge Me Not on ALLBLK on Thursday, May 25, 2023.
