Who is TikTok couple Lunden Stallings and Olivia Bennett?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

LUNDEN Stallings and Olivia Bennett gained fame via social media.

The popular TikTok couple tied the knot on Saturday, September 30, 2023.

Who is Lunden Stallings?

Lunden Stallings is a social media star who gained popularity with her now-wife, Olivia Bennett.

The 26-year-olds have over 703K followers on their TikTok account, @lundenandolivia.

As People notes, the pair are popular for their "day in the life," "outfit of the day," and wedding planning videos.

The fashion influencers also share an LTK account, @oliviaandlunden, as well as an Amazon page.

Lunden has 225K followers on her Instagram account, @lunden.rose, and also runs a ShopMy page.

According to her LinkedIn profile, she works in Atlanta, Georgia as an enterprise account executive for SHI International Corp.

She studied at the University of Central Arkansas from 2015 to 2019, earning her bachelor's degree in broadcast journalism.

Who is Olivia Bennett?

Like Lunden, Olivia is also a social media influencer.

She has more than 68K followers on her TikTok page, @oliviamccollumben.

Per her LinkedIn profile, she works as a family specialist for Georgia HOPE in Roswell, Georgia.

She received her bachelor's degree in social work from Jacksonville State University, where she studied from 2015 to 2019.

Olivia then attended the University of Alabama from 2020 to 2021, earning her master's degree in social work.

When did the couple get married?

Lunden and Olivia were married on Saturday, September 30, 2023, per People.

The wedding took place at Naylor Hall, a "historic venue" in Roswell, Georgia.

The duo spoke with the publication before their big day.

Referencing their first look of the ceremony, Olivia said: "We’re both so excited, as two women, to experience each other’s 'big bridal moment.'"

Lunden said: "I know that it's hard to take in a moment, but I do feel like the one thing that Olivia and I are good at is being still. We like romanticizing the moment that we're in. I’m so excited to see her."

People also reported that the pair planned the whole wedding themselves with assistance from Lunden's mother, Ashley Gilbow.

Lunden said: "The whole wedding party is in white."

Olivia added: "The guys [brothers and fathers] are in white dinner jackets with black pants, and then all of the 12 bridesmaids are in ivory, satin gowns."

She continued: "We decided to make our moms our something blue. Miss Ashley is wearing a stunning blue gown, and when I walk down the aisle, I’m going to hold blue hydrangeas for my mom who passed away.

"My dad will walk me down and my mom will be the first aisle seat in the front row, so I will put those flowers in my mom’s chair, and then I’ll walk back up the aisle with my white bridal bouquet."
