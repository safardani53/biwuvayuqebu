Yankee Candle launch Halloween collection including Trick or Treat and Haunted Hayride scents Th

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 5, 2024

YANKEE Candle has just launched its spooktacular Halloween collection featuring new scents including Trick or Treat and Haunted Hayride.

With October 31st just around the corner, it’s time to think about your costume and putting up some scary decorations.

As well as making your home look frightening, this year it can even smell like Halloween.

Yankee Candle’s new range features the colours of the season – orange and purple - and spooky packaging.

The Trick or Treat candle is described online as: “A crisp autumn night filled with the sweet anticipation of sugary treats and spellbinding scents.”

While the Haunted Hayride fragrance is a “hauntingly aromatic” blend of charcoal, black pepper and nutmeg.

  • Trick or Treat, Yankee Candle, £23.99 – buy now

All the scents come in their classic large jar for £23.99, and you can also get a pack of tea lights for £6.99, or a small candle for £1.99.

And you can also pick up a ghost–shaped tea light holder for £7.99, which doubles up as a Halloween decoration.
