Sean Garrett Biography

Sean Garrett was born on a Friday, March 30, 1979 in Atlanta. His birth name is Sean Garrett and he is currently 44 years old. People born on March 30 fall under the zodiac sign of Aries. His zodiac animal is Goat.

Garrett Hamler, better known by his stage name Sean Garrett, is an American singer, songwriter and record producer. He has produced eighteen number one singles in only seven years of producing music. That number puts him in fifth place on the Billboard list of producers with the most number one hits. Garrett is the first producer to achieve this measure of success in under a decade since George Martin, who produced 20 number one hits for The Beatles in 6 1/2 years from 1964 to 1970. He is also the only active hip hop producer that is mentioned on the Billboard list of producers with the most number one hits.

He earned an A.A. in business from the University of Maryland’s program in Germany. He worked as a mortgage broker before turning to music.
