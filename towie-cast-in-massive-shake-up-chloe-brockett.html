Towie cast in massive shake up as stars place on show hanging in the balance

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

A TOWIE cast shake-up could be on the horizon with one star's place on the cast left hanging in the balance.

It was recently announced that the epic ITVBe reality show would be returning for a further two editions in 2024, including a lavish trip to Bali, but not everyone is yet confirmed to be starring on the show.

Sources have exclusively told The Sun that Chloe Brockett's place on the show remains up in the air with bosses yet to make a decision on if she will be back on the programme next year.

An insider told us: "Chloe Brockett still doesn’t know if she’s returning to the show after her suspension.

"The cast haven’t even been told who is going to Bali and who isn’t.

"It’s all hanging in the balance - Chloe makes great TV though so behind the scenes, everyone thinks she’ll go back.”

Chloe was previously suspended from the hit programme over a vicious bust-up.

We told earlier this year how Chloe was suspended after throwing three glasses at co-star Roman Hackett during a furious argument.

A shocked onlooker told The Sun how Chloe exploded with fury at Roman during filming in Brentwood, Essex.

She was immediately pulled off air which put her future on the show into doubt.

With Chloe's appearance on the cast still being up in the air, the rest of the stars are looking forward to being invited on a lavish cast trip for the show when it returns next year.

The broadcaster has recommissioned two more series of the cult reality show after it became the fourth most watched show on ITVX.

Insiders said the huge trip to the tranquil Indonesian island of Bali will be shown on screen over three episodes next year.

A source said: “Towie is back for two new seasons and producers are going all out.

“They are splashing the cash by sending the cast away to Bali for a glamorous trip in February.

“It is the most exotic place they’ve ever experienced to date and is a huge treat for the lucky stars who get booked to go.”
