Who Is Nadine Caridi, Jordan Belfort's Ex-Wife? Where is She Today?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 29, 2024

Nadine Caridi, sometimes referred to as Nadine Belfort or Nadine Macaluso, both last names she married into, came into the spotlight when the news of her divorce from prominent stockbroker husband, Jordan Belfort, surfaced.

However, she took the divorce in stride and started a new marital life with another entrepreneur. Join in to know her current husband, children, and family in detail!

Nadine Caridi's Bio: What Is She Doing Today?

The multi-talented lady was born on Dec. 25, 1967, in London, United Kingdom. She completed her school education at John Dewey High School. 

She received a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Somatic and Depth Psychology from Pacifica Graduate Institute in 2015. Then, she received her post-doctoral training from The NeuroAffective Relational Model.

While pursuing education in the medical field, Caridi also had her hands in modeling from a young age. A young Nadine Caridi became a poster girl of a beer brand, Miller Lite. 

In her younger days, Nadine Caridi also featured in various national campaigns that included the advertisement of ESPN's Premier League football show, Monday Night Football.

But, Caridi quit her burgeoning modeling career to work in the mental health care industry. She started serving as a marriage and family therapist intern in Manhattan Beach Community Counseling from 2011 to 2013.

Today, she serves as a licensed marriage and family therapist in Hermosa Beach, California. She provided help to individuals and couples to create healthy relationships and obtain overall satisfaction in life.

Nadine Caridi's Marriage With Jordan Belfort Ended In 1998

Nadine was first married to Jordan Belfort, an author, motivational speaker, and former stockbroker. Jordan twisted his business like a depraved Roman emperor, where the best employees were rewarded with escorts, drugs, and plenty of alcohol.

His story was narrated in the 2013 movie Wolf of the Wall Street, where his character was portrayed by the Oscar-winning actor Leonardo DiCaprio. Similarly, an Australian actress Margot Robbie essayed the role of Nadine in the movie.

Nadine first met Jordan Belfor when her then-boyfriend, Alan Wilzig, introduced her to him. They got attracted to each other at first glance and started dating, which lasted for several years.

In July 1991, the pair got engaged at a Beach House party. In the same year, they tied the wedding knot after Jordan's divorce from Denise. They went on to welcome two children from their relationship; a daughter Chandler Ann Belfort and a son Carter James Belfort. But, in 1998, they parted ways and opted for a divorce in 2005.

Nadine Caridi Married John Macaluso After Dating For Two Years

Caridi moved on with her divorce and failed marriage. From 1999, she started dating the New York-based entrepreneur, John Macaluso. After dating for two years, she married him on Sep. 30, 2001. 

The couple's wedding came as a surprise as Nadine was 22 years younger than her husband. The age difference didn't amount to much as the duo relish their marriage.

Caridi has two step-children from her husband's previous marriage with LeeAnn Thornton. The daughters are Nikki Macaluso McTernan and Alexandra Macaluso.
