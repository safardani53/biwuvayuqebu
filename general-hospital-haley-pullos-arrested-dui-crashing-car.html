General Hospital star Haley Pullos 'arrested for DUI' after 'crashing car into oncoming traffic on L

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 3, 2024

ACTRESS Haley Pullos has been arrested for a DUI after allegedly causing a major car accident in LA last month.

The 24-year-old is best known for her role as Molly Lansing-Davis on the daytime soap opera General Hospital.

Haley has been arrested for a DUI after allegedly causing a horrific car accident on an LA freeway on April 29, according to TMZ.

In documents obtained by the outlet, police reported that the soap opera star was driving down a Pasadena freeway when she "swerved, flew over the dividing barrier, and into oncoming traffic."

The TV star then collided head-on with another vehicle going about 60 miles an hour in the opposite direction.

Photos from the incident show both cars damaged beyond repair, with shattered windshields and smashed machinery.

After police arrived on the scene of the accident, they claimed that Haley was unable to exit her vehicle due to the extreme damage.

Firefighters were finally able to free her from the car and they helped her into an ambulance.

But according to authorities, when a firefighter was examining her injuries, the actress "struck him" and yelled: "This is a $400 f***ing shirt!"

When her car was later searched by police, weed edibles and mini bottles of tequila were allegedly recovered.

Haley was escorted to the hospital where the cops claimed she showed "clear signs" of driving under the influence, such as slurred speech, watery eyes, and the smell of alcohol on her breath.

The TV personality then allegedly got aggressive and "fought" the hospital staff, leading to her sedation, according to TMZ.

She was arrested shortly after with a felony DUI charge while still under their care.

The driver of the other vehicle reportedly survived the accident but was hospitalized with serious injuries.

The police revealed to TMZ that Haley was also involved in a hit and run collision before her major highway crash, though more details have not been revealed at this time.

HALEY'S STATEMENT

Last week, the young star explained to Soap Opera Digest that she would be taking a break from General Hospital as she recovered from "a car accident."

She told the outlet: "Unfortunately, I was involved in an automobile accident and I’m doing okay, but I am going to need a little time to recover. I will be back as soon as possible!”

Haley's rep told The Sun: "We are still gathering information about what happened and we have no other comments at this time."

TAKING HER PLACE

In her absence,  Holiday Mia Kriegel will be recast in her longstanding role.

The fill-in actress is known for her past performances on Bull and Animal Kingdom.

Haley has held the reoccuring role as Molly on General Hospital since 2009 from the young age of 11.

She also appeared in a pilot episode of Dollhouse, and landed a reoccuring role in Ghost Whisperer as "young Melinda."
