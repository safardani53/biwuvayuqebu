Kim Zolciak Biermann Turks &amp; Caicos Vacation Photos

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 4, 2024

Fun in the Sun

Kim Zolciak Biermann jetted off to Turks & Caicos for a luxurious family trip. During her sun-filled getaway, the Real Housewives of Atlanta alum spent time with her kids, Brielle, 22, Ariana, 17, Kroy Jr. 7, Kash, 6, and 5-year-old twins Kaia and Kane.

And in true Biermann family vacation style, Kim and her older daughters shared plenty of photos of themselves sporting bikinis (or less, in one case) while louging on the sand, jet-skiing, posing on rocks and much more.

In her Au Natural Habitat

"Make up free Sundays #iwokeuplikethis," the Tardy for the Party star captioned a photo taken by her husband Kroy Biermann, in which she posed nude wearing only a floppy sun hat. "I’ve had this outfit for years," she joked.

Itsy Bitsy Teeny Weeny Yellow Bikini

Although it's not a polka dotted number, the reality star showed off her figure (and her long, blonde wig!) with a selfie in a yellow bikini. "Hair Queen! Wig wearing babe for 20 years!! They are Easy, Fun, Versatile and Sexy!!!" she wrote alongside the image. "Coming sooooon going to help so many."

A Butt-Baring Image

Zolciak-Biermann sported a cheeky leapord print swimsuit while her hair was wrapped in a towel as she posed in the sand for an impromptu photoshoot. "Suns out ...... Buns out," she captioned the picture, framed by island flora.

Fun in the Sun

"Magical," the reality star wrote alongside an image of her in a plunging white one-piece with her hair was in a bun beside a fire pit.

A Mother-Daughter Stroll

While walking on the beach with her daughter Kaia, 5, Zolciak-Biermann sported a pink bikini. In the caption, the mom of 5 took the opportunity to thank God for Kaia and her twin brother Kane. "I prayed for you Kaia.. I got buy one get one free " she cheekily wrote. "God’s greatest gift wasn’t surprised .. TWINS run heavy on my Mom’s side!!"

She also used the caption to thank her doctor for her breast reduction, writing, " and a HUGE thank you to @dr.hochstein love my smaller boobs "

Daughter Selfie

After going for a dip, daughters Brielle and Ariana posed for a selfie on the beach while holding what appers to be a juul. "Island Life," Kim wrote alongside the photo of her eldest girls.

A Walk in the Sand

Zolciak-Biermann posted what appears to be a topless photo from behind as she walks down toward the celebrity-frequented island's turquoise water. "Dreaming Blessed and Grateful," she captioned the image, in which she sports a floppy sun hat and a ruffled bikini bottom.

A Dip in the Water

The Bravo star shared a far away photo of herself and her husband standing in the water. "My fav place with my fav," she captioned the brilliant blue photo, giving credit for the shot to daughter Brielle.

Beachy Vibes

Brielle also shared a series of photos from their vacation, including this one where she lies on a beach towel in an orange bikini. "I feel like I’ve literally been kissed by the sun " she wrote.

Sips Under the Sun

While wearing a revealing red bikini and her hair in a bun, Brielle held a tropical drink as she stared out at the ocean. "Feelin good, livin better," she wrote alongside the image.

Sibling Selfie

Brielle snapped a sun-kissed selfie with her sister Ariana and her brother Kroy during a beach day, writing, "Siblings by chance, besties by choice!!!!!!"

Playing in the Water

"Lemme drive da boat," Brielle joked as she hopped on a jet ski in a leapord print bikini.

Selfies for Days

Sporting rose-colored glasses, Zolciak-Biermann shared a selfie with her son Kash as they sat on the sand together. "My (Kash with the good hair)" she wrote, giving a nod to his blonde locks.

Tubing in the Water

Before Kaia set off on an inner tube adventure in the ocean, Kim snapped a photo of her wearing goggles on her head. "Cutie," she wrote alongside the picture.

Tanning by the Sea

Brielle shared an image of herself from behind as she sunbathed on some rocks by the water. In the image, she sports a cheeky striped bikini and her hair is in a fishtail braid: "Sunny & 75 and y’all still have a better view than i do," she joked of the booty-centric snap.

Posing on the Rocks

Ariana shared a picture of herself in a pastel patterned bikini as she stared out into Turks & Caicos's clear blue sea. "The islands," she captioned the photo.

Fun in the Sun

"What’s up beaches," Ariana wrote on a photo of herself as she played with her hair while wearing a green zip-up two-piece while perched on a seating area near the water's edge.

Sibling Best Friends

"Ur my bestie forever ♥️," Ariana said of her younger brother Kane during a sister-brother hang.

Red Hot Bikini

Ariana addressed her boyfriend, who did not appear to be on the trip, in one photo. "Treat me like your number one baby," she wrote, tagging him in the picture of herself in a red bikini standing on the sand.
