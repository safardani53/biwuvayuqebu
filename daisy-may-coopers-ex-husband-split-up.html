Who is Daisy May Cooper's ex-husband and why did they split up?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 22, 2024

THIS Country star Daisy May Cooper has revealed that her lockdown pregnancy shone a light on her marriage difficulties with her now ex-husband Will Weston.

The pair got married in 2019 but only lasted two years due to the pressures of the Covid-19 pandemic.

Who is Daisy May Cooper's ex-husband?

Will Weston is the ex-husband of actress and comedian Daisy May Cooper.

He works as a landscape gardener and owns a company called The Garden & Plant Company.

As of February 2023, he has over six thousand followers on Instagram.

Over on his page, you can see pictures of his adorable children and stunning landscapes.

Find his account under the username @willwestonlandscape

Daisy and Will tied the knot in September 2019 in a small ceremony in The Cotswolds.

Around that time she said in a social media post about their marriage that she could not be happier.

Daisy captioned a photo: “I am lucky to have found my soulmate.”

Why did Daisy May Cooper split from her ex-husband?

Daisy and Will ended their marriage in July 2021.

An insider told The Sun at the time: "Daisy said she had grown increasingly tired of married life and was struggling in her relationship with Will. 

"They ended things for good earlier this year.

"She has taken off her wedding ring and has been telling her friends their marriage is over.

"She has also removed Will as a director on her media business.

"It's been a tough 17 months for everyone and Daisy and Will were no exception."

In September 2022, Daisy opened up about her split from Will and admitted that "there wasn't real love there".

She told The Times: "Will is amazing, an incredible, incredible dad - but fundamentally, there wasn't real love there, I don't think there ever was, really."

She went on to reveal that the lockdown "shone a spotlight" on their marital difficulties" and that she needed to be by herself after the strains of the Covid-19 pandemic.

Do they have any children?

Daisy and Will share two children together; a daughter Pip, and a son, Jack.

The couple announced Daisy was expecting her second child on Instagram in May 2020.

Following the birth of Jack, the pair shared the news on Daisy's social media, posting a picture captioned: "The bubba has landed and he’s absolutely perfect".

Who else has Daisy May Cooper dated?

Following Daisy's split from Will, she then dated chef Ryan Weymouth.

During their time together, Daisy got Ryan's name tattooed on her finger and they even sparked engagement rumours when she was seen with a ring on her wedding finger.

But in May 2022, it was reported that Daisy had "dumped" Ryan after a whirlwind five-month romance.
