See Baracks Daughter In Her Restaurant Uniform Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, June 16, 2024

When you grew up in the White House, working at a restaurant probably isn’t the most exciting way to spend a summer. But Sasha Obama rocked the navy shirt and khaki pants combo with no issue, working the cash register at Nancy’s restaurant on Martha’s Vineyard. In between serving up everything from milkshakes to fried seafood, the President Barack Obama‘s 15-year-old daughter totally owned her summer job! Click here to see pictures of Sasha in her uniform!

Although Sasha looks like just a normal teenage girl working in the pics, it’s quite obvious that she’s somebody important. Not only is it pretty easy to recognize her (even under the baseball cap), but Sasha has six Secret Service agents watching over her at all times!

[jwplatform h40IkGFX]

But despite being the President’s daughter, Sasha has been keeping herself as low-profile as possible. She’s been going by her full name, Natasha, while working the Nancy’s takeout window, and has reportedly been working the four-hour morning shift before Nancy’s opens at 11:30 am. She’ll also apparently be wrapping up the job pretty soon, as the Obamas will be going on their annual summer vacation on Aug. 6.

Sasha seemingly landed her summer gig because her parents have supposedly been friends with the restaurant’s owner Joe Moujabber for years. In fact, the whole Obama family were pictured eating there in 2009, and President Obama was seen there again in 2013. So while working a register may not be the most exhilarating summer job, at least it’s a familiar place for Sasha — and not to mention, it probably has some pretty delicious food!

Sasha & Malia Obama: See Pics Of The President’s Daughters

While Sasha is rocking a pair of khakis and ball cap, her older sister Malia Obama was rocking the complete opposite garments: Tube tops and sports bras. The 18-year-old partied at Chicago’s Lollapalooza music festival at the end of July, and she was even spotted twerking on stage! We’re thinking neither outfits are what the girls will be allowed to wear on vacay with their parents, but Sasha will probably be super happy to get out of that uniform!
