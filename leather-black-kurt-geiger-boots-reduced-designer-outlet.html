FASHION fans go wild as a stunning pair of Kurt Geiger boots have been massively slashed.

So if you’re a fan of Kurt Geiger, the luxury British footwear brand, but can’t afford to splash hundreds of pounds on a pair of shoes (and don’t worry, not many of us can), then you’ll need to listen up.

With temperatures taking a dip, it’s safe to say that many of us have put our flip flops and sandals firmly at the back of our wardrobes and instead swapped for cosy pairs of boots.

One savvy shopper was left stunned when shopping in Kurt Geiger, when they spotted that a £239 pair of black boots had been slashed to only £29 - yes, you heard that correctly.

Thrilled with the discounted find, Daniel Elle took to social media to alert other shoppers.

Posting on Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, a private Facebook group with a staggering 2.3 million members, Daniel shared pictures of their new purchase and penned: “Boundary Mill Colne has lots of Kurt Geiger boots/shoes reduced to £29!”

Not only will the quilted lace-up boots be sure to keep your toes warm in the cold weather, but you’re guaranteed to look fabulous at the same time too.
