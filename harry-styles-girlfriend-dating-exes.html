Harry Styles dating history: Who has the singer dated? The US Sun

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

HARRY STYLES is a world-renowned singer, performer and actor who found fame as part of boyband One Direction.

Though he has racked up countless achievements throughout his career, including Grammy Awards and an Ivor Novello, fans continue to be intrigued by his past romantic life - and who he may be dating now.

Who has Harry Styles dated?

Harry Styles' dating history includes:

Felicity Skinner

Prior to Styles' One Direction fame, he dated Felicity Skinner.

The pair were an item as teenagers for almost a year, but eventually parted ways for an unknown reason.

Skinner said during an interview with the Mirror: "He was a really good boyfriend, very romantic and, yes, shy.

"We were together for just under a year … We just clicked straight away and got on really well.

"He has a really cute smile.

"He was really sweet.

"It was puppy love and we were definitely each other's first loves."

At this time, it is unclear what Skinner is up to now.

Caroline Flack

Syles' first Hollywood relationship was with television presenter and actress Caroline Flack.

The pair, who had a 14-year age gap, dated from 2011 to early 2012, according to People.

It is unclear why they split but Styles revealed that it was a "mutual decision".

Caroline later died in 2020 from suicide.

Taylor Swift

After meeting at the 2012 Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards, Styles began briefly dating music megastar Taylor Swift.

The pair were together from 2013 to 2014.

At first, neither of them talked about their split publicly, but a few months later, Swift seemingly hinted in an interview with Rolling Stone that her songs Style and Out Of The Woods were written about their relationship.

Kendall Jenner

Styles was first linked to Kendall Jenner in 2013 and while they might have never confirmed a relationship, they have been spotted together several times.

It appears that they've remained good friends as she's been spotted at his concerts, and there were rumours that he poured his heart out to her after his split with Olivia Wilde.

Tess Ward

In May 2017, it was revealed that Styles began dating London-based chef and blogger, Tess Ward after being introduced by a mutual friend.

A source told The Sun: “As soon as Harry met Tess there was an instant spark between them.

“They really bonded over their mutual love of quirky fashion and food – and things turned romantic quickly.”

The pair were later pictured together driving around north London in his Audi but split shortly after.

Camille Rowe

After being introduced by mutual friend Alexa Chung in 2017, Styles began dating Victoria's Secret and Louis Vuitton model Camille Rowe.

The pair were spotted out and about in Los Angeles in September 2017, but their relationship came to an end in July 2018.

Olivia Wilde

Olivia Wilde and Styles began dating in January 2021- just weeks after she announced her split from Jason Sudeikis - while on the set of their film Don't Worry Darling.

Their relationship was the talk of the town for over a year and caused a lot of drama, but it was then announced in November 2022 that they were "taking a break."

A source told People: "He's still touring and is now going abroad. She is focusing on her kids and her work in LA. It's a very amicable decision."

While they might have parted ways, the source claimed that they are "still very close friends."

Is Harry Styles single?

As far as the public knows, Harry is single as of July 2023 but there are always rumours circulating about his love life.

Since Styles' 2022 split from Wilde, he has kept his dating life out of the spotlight.

In March 2023, he was reportedly caught "passionately kissing" Emily Ratajkowski in Tokyo while there for his Love On Tour.

Fans speculated that they had become an item but it was unclear if they were ever in a relationship, and haven't been seen together since.

Who is Harry Styles dating now?

Taylor, who has acted opposite Timothee Chalamet and Sterling K. Brown, was spotted in the VIP section of one of the star's concerts in early July 2023.

The actress was seen in the audience in Vienna, Austria, during one of Styles' Love on Tour concerts.

The following day, videos surfaced showing the pair walking through the streets of Vienna together.

Prior to the Harry and Taylor rumours, he had been linked to  influencer and fashion blogger Yan Yan Chan.
