Kailyn Lowry: Makes Sex Video With Other Men!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, June 21, 2024

Kailyn Lowry and Javi Marroquin have been the subject of cheating and divorce rumors for the past several weeks, with a new scandal emerging every few days.

But if Kailyn is bothered by them, she sure ain’t showing it.

In a new video posted to Twitter and Instagram, Kailyn stars in a short with two guys who are vying for her affection.

She starts out getting close to one of the men, but when he makes a crude joke she switches teams and appears on the arm of his friend.

Suitor number two then grabs a condom (magnum, of course) and he heads into the bedroom with Kail.

The video appears to be a comedic short from a couple of amateur filmmakers who produce "The Turt & Ron Show."

And while it is likely a work of fiction, many fans have deemed it inappropriate since Kailyn is married.

"Am I the only one who thinks this is a little inappropriate bc she’s married :/" tweeted one fan.

"This is so wrong unless he don’t give a f**kk," wrote another.

However, others defended the Teen Mom 2 star, reasoning that she was just acting and that everyone should just lighten up.

This new vid only adds fuel to the rumors that Kail and Javi’s marriage is on the rocks – a topic that has attracted much speculation since he was deployed overseas with the U.S. Air Force in January.

The goss heated up last month after Javi tweeted about a racy photo he sent to a lady friend, which many interpreted as an admission of infidelity.

Kailyn herself expressed chagrin over the post in a tweet of her own, writing, "Borderline inappropriate but it is what it is."

She deleted the post moments later.

The pair unfollowed each other on Twitter and Instagram shortly thereafter, leading fans to believe they were officially dunzo.

Since then, Kailyn hung out at the MTV Movie Awards with her ex Jo Rivera (sans wedding ring), and last week was suspected of cheating with another ex, Jordan Wenner.

We don’t know what’s really going on with Kail and Javi, but it’s looking pretty grim from where we’re standing.
