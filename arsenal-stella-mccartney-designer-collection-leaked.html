ARSENAL'S new collection designed by Stella McCartney has been LEAKED online.

A number of shirts, hoodies and leggings are expected to be released, as well as a pair of trainers.

McCartney has been a partner of Arsenal's kit sponsor Adidas since 2004.

And last year McCartney and Manchester United ace Paul Pogba teamed up for the launch of his signature Predator football boots.

One of Arsenal's shirts seen on Footy Headlines and ArsenalShirt will be a primarily red and blue animal print design.

Other shirts, hoodies and leggings have been seen in white and blue.

And shoes will be primarily white with red animal print on the sole of the shoe.

But fans are far from impressed having already seen other collections released this season.
