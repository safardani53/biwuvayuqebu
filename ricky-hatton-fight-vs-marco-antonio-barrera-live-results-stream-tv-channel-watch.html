Ricky Hatton fight vs Marco Antonio Barrera LIVE RESULT: Latest reaction as both men's hands raised

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, May 23, 2024

Mayweather 'no' to Hatton rematch

Should Ricky Hatton fancy another rematch, it won't be with Floyd Mayweather.

The pair came to blows in the ring in 2007 - with 'Money' beating the Mancunian.

But Mayweather isn't interested in doing it all over again.

"Why do it for fake? Ricky Hatton we have had a chance. The great thing at that particular time is that he was at the top of his game. I have always been at the top of my game.

"We were able to come together and get fans all around the world excited.

"Ricky Hatton is one hell of a champion and a UK legend and it was in 2007 when we gave the world what they wanted to see. Action."

Always game for a laugh

Whatever you made of Ricky Hatton's comeback at 44, you can't help but laugh at his ring entrance.

The Hitman was game to poke fun at himself and his age by entering the ring with a zimmer frame.

And he was accompanied for the walk-in by two nurses, of course.

Hatton's son 'well behind' dad's comeback

Before his dad took Ricky Hatton made his comeback last night, son Campbell revealed he was "well behind" the return,

"When I spoke to him about it he told me how it's gonna be carried out,' the 21-year-old told Sportsmail.

"Obviously it's not a proper professional comeback, it's an exhibition with Barrera and everything's being done fairly. 

"We saw the one with [Evander] Holyfield [against Vitor Belfort] and mismatches like that are where it's wrong, but this is a really fair matchup and my dad's in great shape now. Marco looks to be in a similar place and he's looking really well.

"It's all being done correctly and it's going to a good cause - so I was well behind it. And seeing the benefits it's had on my dad, not just physically but where he is mentally, he's in a really good place, he's got that motivation and he's living really well, it can't be anything but a good thing for him.'
