Rapper Duki was born on June 24, 1996 in Buenos Aires, Argentina (He's 27 years old now).

Rapper who rose to notoriety after competing in the El Quinto Escalón Argentine freestyle rap scene. He went on to become a significant character in Argentine trap music with songs like "Hello Cotto" and "She Don't Give a FO". In 2018, he joined the hip hop group Band #ModoDiablo.
