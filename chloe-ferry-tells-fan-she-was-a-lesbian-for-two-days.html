Geordie Shore star Chloe Ferry tells fan she was a lesbian 'for two days'

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, May 2, 2024

CHLOE Ferry has revealed that she was briefly a lesbian after a fan admitted that she had a crush on her.

The 23-year-old took to Twitter to reply to the fan who had called the Geordie Shore star one of her "biggest inspirations and lesbian crushes".

"@Chloe_GSHore is one of my biggest inspirations and lesbians crushes I have. I just can't deal. Am not even sorry," commented the fan.

Chloe quickly replied: "I used to be a Lesbian for 2 days."

Chloe is currently in a relationship with her Geordie Shore co-star Sam Gowland, 23, who she has been with since October 2017.

However, shortly before the bubbly blonde started dating Sam, she was filmed enjoying on screen snogs with Marnie Simpson, and was even spotted getting steamy under the covers with Charlotte Crosby on the MTV show in 2016.

Chloe later revealed that she and Charlotte had even started a brief relationship, but they ended it when they realised they "didn’t like girl-on-girl" sex.

She told The Sun Online: "I tried having a relationship with a girl for two days, but when I tried to have sex with her I realised I didn’t like it.

"The girl was Charlotte Crosby, no one knows about it, we were mainly just drunk and carrying on.

"We tried to have a relationship but we just didn’t like it as we realised we weren’t lesbians."

However, a representative for Charlotte later denied the claims that the pair were involved.

They said: "Chloe says the most random things at times! The 'lesbian experience' can only be the drunken tongue twister on Geordie Shore, which was on screen over two years ago."

But despite her past antics, the Geordie star recently admitted that she is ready for the "next chapter" of her life with boyfriend Sam.

She told The Sun Online in October: "I'm coming off my contraception so I can fall pregnant.
