Stars Meeting Santa in 2022, Photos

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, March 7, 2024

Kaley Cuoco & Tom Pelphrey

Kaley Cuoco and Tom Pelphrey are already making holiday memories with their baby girl.

The Ozark actor shared photos on Instagram on Dec. 22 showing a fun-filled day of maternity photos alongside the Fight Attendant actress, who is expecting their first baby. The parents-to-be posed with Santa, as well as members of Cuoco's family, in the festive holiday photos, captioned with seasonal emojis.

Liberty Domenech

Meghan McCain is celebrating her last Christmas before becoming a girl mom of two!

The pregnant former View co-host shared a series of festive family photos from the holiday on Instagram, featuring daughter Liberty and Santa Claus.

In the cute shots, McCain, who is expecting her second baby girl with husband Ben Domenech this winter, brought her little girl to say hi to the man in red.
