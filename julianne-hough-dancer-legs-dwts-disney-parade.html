Julianne Hough shows off dancer legs as she twirls in a bright red Valentino mini-dress for hosting

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, March 23, 2024

JULIANNE Hough has mesmerized fans by flaunting legs for days in a striking red Valentino dress on Monday.  

The Dancing With The Stars pro urged Instagram followers to catch the Disney Parks Magical Christmas Day Parade on ABC, which she co-hosted with her brother Derek.

Julianne, 35, twirled in slow motion, showcasing the flowing, high-cut dress paired with red stilettos and a bold red lip. 

Standing before Disney World's iconic Cinderella castle, she exuded both sexiness and, of course, magic in the captivating video.

In the caption, she playfully asked: "Who needs a white Christmas when you could have a Valentino Christmas?" 

Encouraging fans to "tune in to all the holiday magic," she shared that the parade would air on Monday at 10 AM ET and be available on Hulu afterward.

'LEGS ALL DAY!'

Fan reactions poured in, appreciating Julianne's stunning appearance: "The legs are legging!!" 

Another wrote: "Legs all day!" 

A third posted: "Gorgeous," and a fourth: "stunning!" 

A fifth was more critical and wrote: "Prefer a white Christmas, but you look stunning."

The two-hour event was co-hosted by Mickey Guyton and Jesse Palmer at Disneyland Resort in California. 

The star-studded parade featured performances by Ariana DeBose, Chrissy Metz, The Smashing Pumpkins, Iam Tongi, Meg Donnelly, Michael Bolton, and the Broadway and North American Tour cast of Disney's Aladdin.

Some fans ripped the special as "lazy" because most of it was pre-taped with a "minute of the parade," it also featured throwback clips because it was the 40th anniversary.

SURGERY UPDATE

The special comes during a difficult time for the brother and sister dancers.

It was taped in early November, before Derek's wife Hayley's scary second emergency craniectomy.

Last Thursday, the 38-year-old announced that Hayley's second surgery was successful.

"With immense relief, gratitude and overwhelming joy I’d like to share that Hayley’s cranioplasty surgery has been successfully completed as planned," he wrote in an Instagram post.

"My sincere appreciation goes to the exceptional medical team. Their expertise and steady hands were pivotal in this journey, ensuring a smooth and successful surgery.

"Especially Dr. Mai who not only performed the surgery but was also the one who saved her life 2 weeks ago."

The Dancing With the Stars judge said that he and Hayley are grateful for the love and support they've been receiving.

"Your thoughts, prayers, and positive energy have been a source of strength and comfort for us during this challenging time," he wrote.


'PRAYERS PLEASE'

On December 7, Derek let his followers know about what happened to Hayley after a stop on their tour in Washington, DC the night before.

"At the end of last night's tour performance in Washington D.C., my wife, Hayley became disoriented and was taken to the hospital," he started off the post.

"She was diagnosed with a cranial hematoma from a burst blood vessel and required an emergency craniectomy."

He said in the Instagram post that Hayley was in stable condition.

"I want to thank the first responders and medical personnel who have cared for and continued to care for her," Derek said.
