Vanderpump Rules Season 3 Episode 7 Recap: Revenge of the Stassi

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

On Vanderpump Rules Season 3 Episode 7, the gang celebrated gay pride week in West Hollywood, Lisa hired a new hostess and Stassi was back with a vengeance.

Yes, Stassi Schroeder has been back in L.A. throughout the third season, but only on Vanderpump Rules Season 3 Episode 7 did we see old, pot-stirring ways return.

Also, Kristen Doute and Tom Sandoval butted heads, and HARD.

Not literally, of course … but it nearly came to that for the exes.

Watch Vanderpump Rules Season 3 Episode 7 Online

Stassi Schroeder, as you know if you watch Vanderpump Rules online, was once de facto queen of this crew. Now that she no longer works at SUR, however?

She’s just the drama queen who meddles in everybody’s lives and somehow keeps hanging around the restaurant(s) despite not even having a job there.

Monday, we saw Stassi try to persuade Katie Maloney not to be so quick to forgive Tom Schwartz, and to make Schwartz dump Jax Taylor as a friend.

 

She also said Scheana Marie’s over-the-top eyebrows looked like two butterflies landed on them and s–t on a crayon. Hey, it was pride week, come on.

Stassi’s alleged boyfriend must not be putting out as often or as well as she’d like, because she certainly has a lot to say about other people’s relationships.

We’re starting to question if that “boyfriend” is even real, come to think of it. People have been known to “date” sex toys and/or porn before. It makes you think.

Tom Schwartz, also known as Captain Passive, doesn’t seem convinced that Stassi has Katie’s best interests in mind, or that Jax poses a real threat here.

“Contrary to what Stassi believes, not everything’s about Jax, okay. More importantly, not everything’s about you, Stassi,” Tom said, and very little changed.

Meanwhile, Kristen Doute and Tom Sandoval were the other featured couple of the week, which is funny since they broke up like an eternity ago now.

She just can’t escape him, even though he seems happy with Ariana Madix. Maybe because of that reason. Either way, her mail is still going to his place! OMG!

Time to make a histrionic appearance to go over there and retrieve it. Knowing something was up, Tom invited his namesake over over to act as backup.

She certainly seemed like hooking up was on her mind, if not her goal. She told new boyfriend James she “might have a nip slip” in the outfit she had picked out.

Because that’s normal. Anyway, it turned into a big fight. Obviously. She accused him of cheating, he denied it, tears were shed and so much drama unfolded.

So much unnecessary drama, it goes without saying. Tom appears to have moved on, and Kristen is dating someone new too. Why the deja vu all over again?

Speaking of moving on, Jax proclaimed after 10 seconds of meeting new hostess Vail that he’s “going to sleep with her, probably.” We would expect nothing less.
