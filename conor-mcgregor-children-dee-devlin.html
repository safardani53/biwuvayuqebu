How many children does Conor McGregor have and how old are they?

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, March 24, 2024

CONOR McGregor will be hoping to make his return to MMA at the huge UFC 300 show in 2024.

When he is not fighting in the ring, the Irishman devotes his time to his growing family. 

How many children does Conor McGregor have?  

Conor McGregor is the proud father of four children with fiancee Dee Devlin.

The couple have been together since 2018.

Conor Jr 

Conor Jr is the UFC star's firstborn.

He was born on May 6, 2017, and looks set to follow in his father’s footsteps.

McGregor revealed he’s been training the young boy right from the start in the hopes he will have a professional career in sports which he said is a pivotal part of development. 

Speaking at a Q&A event in Glasgow, McGregor said: “When he grows up, I'm going to put him into sport. 

“I believe sport is the best thing you can do. I've already begun training him. He's already leaps and bounds ahead of schedule." 

Croia

Croia is Dee and Conor's only daughter and second child.

She was born in January 2019. 

The name Croia derives from the Irish word of croi meaning heart. 

Rian 

Rian is the couple's third child, born in May 2021. 

Announcing the birth of their third child, McGregor wrote on Instagram: “Healthy baby boy delivered! Baby and Mammy Wonder Woman are doing great! 

“God I thank you for everything you give to me and my family in this world. 

“My new born son, Rian McGregor.” 

Conor McGregor announced on X on November 30th that his and Dee Devlin's fourth child had been born.

The UFC icon posted: "My baby boy is born! Mammy and baby doing amazing, 8.1lbs of prime Irish double champion beef!"

Who is Conor McGregor's fiancée Dee Devlin? 

Dee Devlin is a businesswoman who met McGregor in a Dublin nightclub. 

The couple have known each other since 2018 with McGregor popping the question to her on August 9, 2020, on her 33rd birthday. 

What is Conor McGregor's net worth? 

McGregor has an estimated net worth of £160million ($200million).  

According to Celebrity Net worth, his money includes the estimated over £80million from his August 2017 fight against Floyd Mayweather, as well as £40million from his October 2018 fight against Khabib Nurmagomedov.
