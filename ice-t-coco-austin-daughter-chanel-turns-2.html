Ice-T and Coco Austin's Daughter Chanel Turns 2

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, April 18, 2024

Happy birthday to Ice-T and Coco Austin‘s daughter Chanel Nicole!

The Law & Order: SVU actor’s youngest child turned 2 on Tuesday. The proud parents celebrated her big day with princess attire as seen in a photo shared by the mother of one.

“My baby girl turned 2 years old today! Being a mom is so much fun to see them grow! Happy Birthday Chanel!” Austin, 38, captioned a picture of her toddler dressed in a pink tutu, sequined crown and white shirt that read “It’s My 2nd Birthday” while sitting on a gold throne.

Austin also shared some throwback photos from when Chanel was born.

“2 years ago today I walked into @englewoodhospital and had @babychanelnicole .. True love is real! Perfect in every way,’ Austin captioned the photos.

As seen on invite‘s shared on Chanel Nicole’s social media accounts, the birthday girl had a Paw Patrol-themed bash to mark her second birthday.
