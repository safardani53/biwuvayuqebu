Longchamp tips: Templegate's best bets for blockbuster Sunday in France with stellar Arc de Triomphe

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 4, 2024

TEMPLEGATE turns his eye to all the top class racing taking place in Paris this weekend.

Our top tipster runs through the main events on Sunday and reveals his pick in each.

1.15

AGARTHA ran a cracker to finish second in the Group 1 Moyglare Stud at The Curragh 21 days ago. She relished soft when taking the Debutante at the same track and looks ready for a mile.

Fleur D’Iris was an easy Group 3 winner here last time and is proven at the trip.

Zellie chased her home that day and has an each-way shout again. Raclette is taking a big hike in class but was impressive at Chantilly.

1.50

ANCIENT ROME stepped up to this mile for an easy win here last time. That was just his fourth run and there’s more to come. Angel Bleu took the Vintage Stakes at Goodwood and Frankie Dettori stays on board with every chance.

Stone Age went down in a head-bobber for the Champions Juvenile Stakes at Leopardstown and has potential for Aidan O’Brien.

Ebro River ran a cracker in the National Stakes at The Curragh but this trip on soft ground will be a stretcher.
