Extremely rare 1979 Ford Escort known as a '70s legend' hits auction for eye-watering price

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, February 26, 2024

AN extremely rare 1979 Ford Escort known as a "70s legend" has hit auction for an eye-watering price.

The red 1600 sport MK2 motor which "holds a legendary reputation across the globe" has been restored and is ready to find a new owner.

It was imported to the UK last year from the family who owned it in South Africa.

Currently based in Dublin, the car has had the same owner since it was bought brand new in 1974.

During its restoration the vehicle was given new shocks and springs, a new carburettor, a full new exhaust system, new gearbox & engine mountings, new brakes and new fuel lines.

It was also given an engine rebuild.
